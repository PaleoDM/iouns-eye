An elderly human toymaker who created Maligno, the first carrionette. Carries profound parental guilt over what his creation became. Cannot bring himself to condemn Maligno despite knowing his nature. Holds a music box that reveals visions of the past. Last seen walking into the theater to "repair" Maligno after the party's battle (Session 41).
