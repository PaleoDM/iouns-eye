Tyrell's human father (adoptive), living in Tyr'Zareth. Gruff, hard-knocks background. Wishes Ty were more like Thorn — straightforward and heroic. Not to be confused with Cliff Tuskegee, the loxodon janitor.

Revealed in Session 46 (via Captain Alistair) to have been the master accountant behind an underground railroad that smuggled slaves out of drow communities in the Underdark. He cooked the books well enough to fool Rifthaven's accountants while Roe ran the apothecary that kept Alistair's ship legally employed. A freedom fighter hiding behind ledgers.
