Military officer who commanded the Silverweave Legion's operation in Boughshadow to commodify the Tree of Ambition. Originally from Pearl Bay, he bonded with a sea cucumber as a youth and became obsessed with exploitation. Operated an anti-ward apparatus to breach Nanny Pu'Pu's protections. Carried a poisoned/charming pistol. Killed during the party's ambush (Session 36).
