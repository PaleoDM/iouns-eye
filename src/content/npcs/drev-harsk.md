An Aroiin war mage and professional military commander assigned to the TTT. Middle-aged, career-minded, and fundamentally decent beneath his mercenary pragmatism. When he witnessed a chain devil materialize in the compound (Session 29) and was ordered to conquer Ishetar and seize an infant, he refused. "I can't do any of this," he declared (Session 30), switching allegiance to the party.

In Session 31, the party teleported to his family estate, Manor Harsk, to extract his wife Isla, oldest daughter Lisel, middle son Haven, and youngest daughter Rekka before the Guildmasters could use them as leverage. They found the estate seized by Callista Vohn and the headservant Bessa executed. The extraction succeeded on all three fronts. Drev tearfully modified his loyal stablemaster Udo's memory ("Operation Mindflayer") so Udo would believe the entire family perished in the fire Lady Isla set as they escaped. The family now travels with the party toward Ishetar.
