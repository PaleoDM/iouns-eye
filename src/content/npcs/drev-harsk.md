An Aroiin war mage and professional military commander assigned to the TTT. Middle-aged, career-minded, and fundamentally decent beneath his mercenary pragmatism. When he witnessed a chain devil materialize in the compound (Session 29) and was ordered to conquer Ishetar and seize an infant, he refused. "I can't do any of this," he declared (Session 30), switching allegiance to the party. He has family in Aroiin territory at risk from Guildmaster Lucan Tesseri's threats.
