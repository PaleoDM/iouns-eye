Headservant of Manor Harsk and wife to Udo the stablemaster. Dedicated 224 years of service to the Harsk family. Executed by Lady Callista Vohn's soldiers and piked in the courtyard as a message to the household, accused of conspiring with Brenna Tolvane. Vicas and Azrael brought her body down from the pike during the extraction. Udo refused to leave her side.
