A Rakshasa fiend summoned by the Hushed Choir's ritual (Session 11) who rose to control the House of Coinwrights — Rifthaven's most powerful financial institution. Orchestrated yugoloth mercenary attacks against the party during the Roc Run (Session 16). Appeared in disguise at the victory banquet to deliver subtle threats (Session 17). Commands fiendish powers and mercenary contracts. The party's primary recurring antagonist.

By the end of Session 50, all three of Haji's "stools" of leverage in Rifthaven had been knocked out. The Pallbearer was deposed, Vine Ashen Bloom and the Aletheia Procedure were exposed, and the Cassini Brothers were dead with their 111 collateral souls recovered. With no infrastructure left to move souls to the Hells, Haji is isolated.
