Known as "The Timbermaster," Brenna Tolvane is the leader of the Turquoise Timber Trust and a corrupted druid who uses nature magic for industrial exploitation. She employs reversed Plant Growth, Speak with Plants for intelligence, and beast spies for surveillance. Politically shrewd and pragmatic, she oversaw the environmental destruction of the Myreshade Forest. When the party exposed Verlaine Luthaire as a Celestial Seed agent in her compound, Tolvane fled south on a giant eagle (Session 29).
