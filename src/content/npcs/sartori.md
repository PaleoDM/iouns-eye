A sapient automaton (RELIC, designation Beta07) who operates Sartori's Arcane Outfitters, a high-fashion magical armory. Recruited the party to rescue other RELICs (Rust, Clubs, Rider, Spirit) from the House of Coinwrights' operations (Session 18). Established a covert alliance with the party while maintaining an infiltrator presence within the Coinwrights. Provided powerful potions to the party.
