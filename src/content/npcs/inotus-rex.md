Inotus Rex is the Necromancer King, a powerful undead spellcaster operating within the Fractured Expanse. He raised Dr. Nastase and her academic expedition as undead servants and seeks the mausoleum of great archmages. His mark — a pale jawless skull with acid green eyes — identifies his creations. He can see and hear through his undead servants.
