The collective consciousness of a myconid fungal colony living in the tunnels beneath the Sparkworks District. Was previously fragmented after aberrations invaded the tunnels, forcing the Great Self to isolate parts of itself. Restored to wholeness by the party (Session 7). In exchange for protection, the colony maintains the tunnel infrastructure — bioluminescent glow-dome lighting, shrieker alarm systems, and grown fungal bridges across the underground chasm. Granted Thorn the Cap of Mycelium. Individual members include Speak (communicator), Care (healer), Spear, and Acid.
