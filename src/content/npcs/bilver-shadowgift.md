A former colleague of Quin's. Bilver was dead but his soul was "still pupating" in a chrysalis tank in the lower levels of the High Haven Arcane Academy, partially reconstituted under Gideon's supervision.

In Session 47, Gideon suggested Bilver and Quin settle their dispute about the Aletheia Procedure scientist to scientist. The two argued about grant funding and who was the better researcher. Bilver eventually snapped, broke free of the tank, and attacked with a powerful howl. Veil grappled the creature and Freddie deployed an antimagic field. Veil and Thorn finished him off.

Bilver's notes contained plans for an agricultural revolution using undead labor and "reverse mortgages for your bodies," where citizens lease their corpses after death to clear debts.
