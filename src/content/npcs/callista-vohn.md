A goliath noblewoman and tribal leader from the Astral Peaks. Arrived at Manor Harsk under orders from the Aroiin Guildmasters with soldiers and papers declaring a conspiracy involving Brenna Tolvane. Her actual agenda was to secure a marriage between herself and Lisel Harsk, consolidating land holdings in the foothills. Her tribe committed warriors against Oglin in exchange for the Harsk estate from the Empire. Executed the headservant Bessa as a message to the household and placed Lady Isla and Lisel under house arrest. Beaten badly by Vicas and Azrael during the party's extraction of the Harsk family (Session 31).
