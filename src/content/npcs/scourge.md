A young green dragon hatched from eggs in the Boughshadow cavern pools. Three of her siblings were raised as undead and killed by the party; Scourge survived and allied with them. Manipulative and ambitious despite her claims of modesty. Made a compact with the party and now serves as guardian of Boughshadow alongside Nanny Pu'Pu. Her poison breath proved decisive in the ambush against Silverweave forces.
