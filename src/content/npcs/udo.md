Ancient stablemaster and groundskeeper at Manor Harsk, husband to Bessa. Served the Harsk family for 224 years alongside his wife. When the party arrived to extract the Castellan's family, Udo nearly shot Drev with a crossbow out of fear. Declared himself "ride or die" for the Castellan. Refused to leave the manor after Bessa's death, cradling her body. At his request ("Operation Mindflayer"), Drev modified his memory so he would believe the entire Harsk family and the Ishetar Guardians perished in the blaze, giving him plausible deniability against truth spells.
