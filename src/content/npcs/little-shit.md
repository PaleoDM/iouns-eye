An imp employed at the customs docks of the Potter's Field, known for a terrible disposition and an excellent network of contacts throughout the domain. Hired by Tyrell for five Plebeians as a guide in Session 43. He offered to assist the party's heist of the Galleria in exchange for one of two payments: the right to take Leon's skin as his own, or passage to the material plane. He chose the latter — he dreams of becoming a dermatologist.

During the escape in Session 45, stopped to renegotiate. His competing allegiances to the Pallbearer and the party were pulling him apart. He needed a firmer deal to override the Pallbearer's claim. The party agreed to write letters of recommendation for him on the material plane, and Veil very hesitantly agreed to mentor him in spying.
