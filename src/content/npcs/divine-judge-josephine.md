One of the Seven High Justicars of the Aletheia Procedure. Served as the Pallbearer's agent in Potter's Field, hunting for Quin's soul. Encountered and defeated by the party in the Actuarium (Session 43), with Leon landing the killing blow. Her death confirmed the conspiracy between the Justicar order and the Pallbearer's soul trade.
