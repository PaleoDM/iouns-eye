A monk of the Order of the Ju-Shi and an old friend of Veil's. "Ghost" is her monk name and the only one she goes by.

In Session 48, Ghost took down a guard at the sealed Ju-Shi monastery with a poison dart and met up with Veil and the rest of the party. She led them past the Coinwright mercenary muscle patrolling the halls with a Pass Without a Trace, briefed them on Vine Ashen Bloom's charges against Sunder, and announced them into the tribunal chamber as Sunder's legal counsel. She took an immediate shine to Ty's leather pants and Goth affect, and pre-emptively shushed him before he could ruin the mystique.
