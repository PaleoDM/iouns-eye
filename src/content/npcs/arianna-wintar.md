Arianna Margaster Wintar is the High Magister of Kalari who led Project Anima, a program to create soul-bearing constructs. She married Lord Byron Wintar and wielded immense arcane power. She is currently imprisoned in Orbos. Her work with soul-binding is considered a transgression against divine law and contributed to priests losing their magic after the Fall of Old Perile Falls.
