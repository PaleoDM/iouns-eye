The Darklord of Odaire, a Domain of Dread in the Shadowfell. A sentient carrionette (animated puppet) created by the toymaker Guiseppe. Desperately seeks to be "real" and enforces control through games and rules over dozens of trapped children. Has a child-king mentality craving validation. Defeated by the party (Session 41) — bound by Chromatic Orb — but left alive. His calling card led the party to Potter's Field.
