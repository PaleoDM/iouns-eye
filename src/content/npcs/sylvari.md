Silver-haired elf with moonlit grace, co-founder of the Restful Lily sanctuary. Was cursed as a medusa for an unknown period. Restored by the party during Sessions 11-12. A devotee of the Moonweaver, she is less politically minded than her sister Solara. Hosted the diplomatic parley with the TTT at the Restful Lily (Session 27).
