A young monk of the Order of the Ju-Shi. In Session 48, Duty was posted on guard at the sealed monastery gates during the lockdown following Sir Sunder's arrest. He was friendly and forthcoming with what little he knew before Ghost dropped him with a poison dart to clear the way into the monastery.
