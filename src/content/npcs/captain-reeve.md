Female mercenary captain formerly employed by the House of Coinwrights. Commanded the Moving Fortress but was captured by the party (Session 19). Bound to Haji through an infernal soul contract, she was highly motivated to escape his service. Offered a redemption deal and now serves as head of Bastion security for the party's Sparkworks operation.

In Session 50, Reeve led the bastion's defense against the Cassini Brothers' diversionary attack on Sparkworks. She held the line while the party pushed past her toward the tunnels.
