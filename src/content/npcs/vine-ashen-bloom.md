A Divine Judge and monk. Identified in Session 46 as the key obstacle Sir Sunder would need to get past in order to invoke a vote of no confidence in the Aletheia Procedure on behalf of the Order of the Ju-Shi.

In Session 48, Vine had Sunder arrested and brought before the monastery tribunal on fabricated charges of colluding with the House of Coinwrights, when she was the one actively doing so. She arrived at the monastery with Coinwright mercenary muscle rather than fellow monks, underscoring the depth of her corruption. Sunder named our heroes as his legal counsel and invoked trial by combat. The heroes cut through her mercenaries and turned the tribunal against her. Sunder and the party laid out damning evidence of her ties to the Coinwrights, the soul trade, and the Pallbearer. The tribunal voted overwhelmingly to prosecute. Vine attempted to escape via dimension door; the spell was counterspelled by Ty and she was taken down before she could act, to be held for a public trial.
