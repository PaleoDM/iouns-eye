A beholder who controlled a massive cavern lair in the Boughshadow underground, featuring floating rock islands and eyestalks. Nearly killed Arden and did kill Quin with his disintegration ray (Session 32). Defeated by the party with Ty landing the final blow. His death ended regional magic effects and yielded a treasure hoard of 26,000 gold pieces and magical items.
