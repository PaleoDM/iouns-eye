One of the original colonists who founded Ishetar. A retired adventurer who now serves as drillmaster at the barracks, training Ishetar's militia alongside Vicas. Friend of Gertrude Earthenstone.

During the Battle of the Gates (Session 32), Hrothgar led the defense of Ishetar against Sallow's forces, personally commanding skirmishes at the walls. When Sallow arrived piloting a tree-mechanical juggernaut, Hrothgar baited it into a dwarven pit trap by standing directly in its path and letting it step on him. The juggernaut was rooted in place.

In Session 33, Hrothgar was confirmed alive at the bottom of the trap, having driven a caltrop on a pike through the juggernaut's foot to pin it in place. He warned the party he could hold for minutes, not hours, dispensed Lay on Hands, and sent them up into the creature.
