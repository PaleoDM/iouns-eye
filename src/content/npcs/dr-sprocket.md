A human artificer and mad scientist specializing in body augmentation with bionic components and memory suppression. Employed by the House of Coinwrights through the Sergente family. Conducted horrific experiments on subjects including Ernesto De la Alma, seeking to create hybrid labor units. Aspires to augment RELICs (sapient constructs) for labor control. Defeated by the party (Session 19) but survived.
