A crystalline quartz dryad spirit who guards the Tree of Ambition — one of seven World Trees. Instructed Thorn on the nature of ambition and gifted him the Lore-Axe, allowing him to "speak for the trees." Told Thorn that "five is too few, seven is better," implying two World Trees have already been compromised.
