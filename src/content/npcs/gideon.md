Veil's brother. Holds a department chair in the school of necromancy at the High Haven Arcane Academy. During the tour of the Pallbearer's Galleria in Session 44, Veil found an empty portrait frame with Gideon's name beneath it. The frame beside it suggested someone had paid to hold the spot — his portrait is not there yet, but there is room for it, reserved and waiting. Veil stole the empty frame on her way out.

In Session 46, Art Doyle identified Gideon as the party's best play at the Academy. As a high-ranking member, he has the authority to invoke a vote of no confidence in the Aletheia Procedure.

In Session 47, the party visited him at High Haven. The reunion with Veil was awkward. Gideon didn't know she or their parents were alive, and Veil wondered if his memory had been modified. He was suspicious of the party's claims about the Aletheia Procedure and suggested Quin and Bilver Shadowgift settle the matter scientist to scientist. After Bilver's creature form attacked and was destroyed, Gideon invoked the vote of no confidence and left with the party.
