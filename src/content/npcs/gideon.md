Veil's brother. During the tour of the Pallbearer's Galleria in Session 44, Veil found an empty portrait frame with Gideon's name beneath it. The frame beside it suggested someone had paid to hold the spot — his portrait is not there yet, but there is room for it, reserved and waiting. Veil stole the empty frame on her way out. His current whereabouts and status in the material plane are unknown.
