The Darklord of Potter's Field, a Domain of Dread that operates as a capitalist afterlife. Runs a soul auction house where the dead are traded as currency. Controls the Seven High Justicars of the Aletheia Procedure. Never breaks a contract and is entirely polite and legal in execution. His personal Galleria contains soul portraits, including Quin's and Art Doyle's. Rides a spirit naga as a summoned steed.

In Session 45, confronted the party directly at the docks when they attempted to escape with stolen soul portraits. Offered to let them leave freely in exchange for Captain Alistair's soul. When refused, engaged in combat alongside four death knights. Killed Leon Empyrean via a Power Word Kill contingency triggered on his death. The party escaped aboard the Dancing Dirge with Quin and Doyle's portraits.
