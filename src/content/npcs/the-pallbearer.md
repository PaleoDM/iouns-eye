The unseen Darklord of Potter's Field, a Domain of Dread that operates as a capitalist afterlife. Runs a soul auction house where the dead are traded as currency. Controls the Seven High Justicars of the Aletheia Procedure. Never breaks a contract and is entirely polite and legal in execution. His personal Galleria contains 113 soul portraits, including Quin's. The party's primary antagonist for the current arc.
