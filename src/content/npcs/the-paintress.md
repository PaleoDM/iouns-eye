A blind artist who works within the Pallbearer's estate. She cannot see, but seems to perceive her subjects with unnerving depth — she knew the shape of Leon's history and the weight of his question before he spoke. She pressed him to stop searching for the truth and simply paint it instead: to decide what the truth would be going forward. When Leon moved toward the canvas, a brush pricked his finger and his blood painted an image of himself at eight years old — battered, adrift, and searching for a home — a child the Aletheia Procedure claimed before he had any say in the matter.
