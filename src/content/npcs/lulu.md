A hollyphant celestial creature who became Tyrell's warlock patron (Session 15), replacing his original aberrant patron "Mini Ty." She glows with subterranean moonlight and is ancient and protective. Can manifest corporeally through the Friendship Locket (Session 18) to cast spells and aid Ty. Facilitated Quin's post-death communion with Ty in Odaire (Session 41). Sister to the Faerie Godmother who watches over Odaire.
