Tyrell's drow mother, living in the underdark city of Tyr'Zareth. Spacey and kind, with a hippie-like personality. Desires Ty to open up emotionally. Part of Ty's adoptive family (Captain Alistair is his biological father).

In Session 50, Ty discovered that the curse Lolth placed on Captain Alistair was not falling on Alistair himself. It was falling on Roe. She is aging unnaturally fast and losing herself slowly. The talisman that breaks the curse is Alistair's old adventuring tricorn, which is not in easy reach.
