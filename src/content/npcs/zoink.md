A tiny, ancient golden dragonborn with a wispy beard and mustache trailing to the floor. Operates the Bank of Zoink, a demiplane-based neutral marketplace where powerful factions, governments, and individuals across Astoria can trade without physically moving goods. His name derives from Bonqka dhiz Zoink, an ancient form of draconic inscribed on the teleporting scale that grants access to his vault. Greeted the party warmly upon their arrival in Session 31 and facilitated their access to the TTT's accounts in Session 32. Also offers teleportation services for a fee.
