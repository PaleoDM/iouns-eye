Wife of Castellan Drev Harsk and lady of Manor Harsk in the Astral Peaks foothills. Placed under house arrest by Callista Vohn along with her oldest daughter Lisel. Extracted by the party in Session 31. Set her own manor ablaze during the escape rather than let the Guildmasters' agents enjoy the comforts of her family home.

At the Bank of Zoink (Session 32), Isla tried to buy her husband's safety by offering a diamond-studded necklace, but the party declined. She proved her value in other ways: she helped the party understand the strategic significance of the TTT gold, explaining that wealth held in Zoink's demiplane didn't need physical transport, opening trade with partners beyond Aroiinae. She specifically named the Leviathan Hunters in the Far North and the Pilgrims Beneath the Diadems as potential allies. A former soldier herself.
