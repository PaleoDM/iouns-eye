A Laenori high elf posing as a trade liaison within the TTT while actually serving as a ranking agent of the Riven Fist — the Celestial Seed cult. Well-dressed, cultured, and effortlessly charming, she always wore gloves to hide the Riven Fist brand on her left palm. During the TTT compound assault (Session 29), she was exposed, summoned a chain devil, and cut Azrael to harvest his Aasimar blood before fleeing south with the sample. She carries a ritual blade inscribed with Convergence liturgy.
