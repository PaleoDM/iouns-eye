A former Divine Judge from years past. His soul was discovered held captive as a portrait in the Pallbearer's private Galleria — a prisoner of the very system he served. The party recovered his portrait in Session 44 alongside Quin's. When freed, he looked at Leon with disdain rather than gratitude.

In Session 45, Doyle spoke with Leon in the space between life and death during the failed resurrection ritual. They debated whether the system was worth saving.

In Session 46, the party sat down with Doyle's portrait to plan their next move. He challenged Leon to prove himself truly good, then explained the Aletheia failsafe: the Order of the Ju Shi and the High Haven Arcane Academy both have the authority to invoke a vote of no confidence, which would freeze the procedure pending governmental review. He pointed the party toward Gideon and Sir Sunder as their best plays at each institution.
