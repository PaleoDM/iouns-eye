Co-leader of the Cassini Brothers crime family alongside his brother Perry Ellis. Oleg ran day-to-day operations and employed enforcers like Major Dunthar.

In Session 50, Oleg and Perry Ellis were caught at the Rum Runner Aquifer attempting to move 111 memory crystals through the Sparkworks Tunnels to deliver to Haji in the Nine Hells. They fielded a crew of thugs and projected a short-range necrotic aura that prevented healing for anyone in melee. Oleg fell to a series of devastating smites from Leon. He was the second of the brothers to die in the engagement.
