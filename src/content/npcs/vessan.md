An ancient celestial being, once a servant of the gods, twisted by centuries of isolation in Ekkorai's post-divine landscape. Gaunt and androgynous with blue-purple slate skin and glowing eyes. They lured the Caldrow family to the oracle shrine intending to use infant Ellie as a vessel — filling her with divinity so she would "rupture" divine energy across the land. Defeated by the party in Session 2 after transforming into a giant elk and divine sparks.
