Youngest daughter of Castellan Drev Harsk. A teenage idealist who holds amateur revolutionary lectures at the local pub, arguing that "monarchy equals poverty" and paying for people's breakfast to get an audience. Her arguments are elementary and somewhat undermined by her own noble birth, but her conviction is genuine. Extracted from town by Lyra and Rooker using Operation Hippogriff in Session 31.
