A couatl guardian set to watch over the divine well north of Ishetar and hold its raw divine magic semi-dormant. After centuries of ward degradation, she was bled nearly to death by Vessan. The party saved her through a complex ritual combining arcane, divine, and primal magic, using divine fire harvested from hell hounds. She is currently resting and recovering under care in Ishetar.
