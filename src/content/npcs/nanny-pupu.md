An elderly witch-like figure who guards the Boughshadow district. Lives in a strange dwelling with a bone-wrought gate. Expert in memory manipulation magic — she predates Dr. Solvane's work. Has a sweet tooth. Made a deal with the party to help protect the Tree of Ambition in exchange for their assistance. Possesses three pairs of Silverweave boots as trophies.
