Elderly matriarch of the De la Alma family and owner of a pulqueria (bar/cantina) in the Salvation District. Formerly a house maid for the Sergente family, she knows all the corridors and secret passages of Sergente Manor. A determined, tactical, no-nonsense leader who organized the rebellion against Lord Sergente's plantation operation. Gifted the party two bottles of powerful Pulque.
