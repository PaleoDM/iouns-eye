Alchemist originally encountered in Emberspost. Brought aboard the Shooting Star by Rooker and traveled with the party through the Firewatch Island campaign. Died during the naval battle at Firewatch but "got better," offering no further explanation. Returned to Ishetar with Captain Alba in Session 26. During the Battle of the Gates (Session 32), Vero held the North Gate alongside Lyra and Sombra.
