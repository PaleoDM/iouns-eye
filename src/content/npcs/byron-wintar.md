Lord Byron Wintar is a historical figure who played a crucial role during the Fall of Old Perile Falls. He used his holy symbol to power the arch-gate, keeping it open so citizens could evacuate the collapsing city. He promised to find Arianna in Orbos, and his fate after the catastrophe remains unknown.
