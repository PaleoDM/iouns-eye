A headless spirit (dullahan) who captains the ghost ship Dancing Dirge in the Shadowfell. His severed head, wreathed in ghostly flame, hangs at his hip. The ship was designed to ferry souls to their rest but now operates as an independent liberation vessel. He doesn't remember his life before becoming the ship's captain, but finds Tyrell oddly familiar — because he is likely Ty's biological father. His appearance resembles an older version of Ty.
