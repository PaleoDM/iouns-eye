A headless spirit (dullahan) who captains the ghost ship Dancing Dirge in the Shadowfell. His severed head, wreathed in ghostly flame, hangs at his hip. The ship was designed to ferry souls to their rest but now operates as an independent liberation vessel. He doesn't remember his life before becoming the ship's captain, but finds Tyrell oddly familiar — because he is likely Ty's biological father. His appearance resembles an older version of Ty.

The Pallbearer's sole demand in Session 45 was Alistair's soul — the one open account on his books. The party refused.

In Session 46, Ty and Alistair finally had the conversation. Alistair shared stories of Cliff and Roe, calling Cliff "Cliffy" and "Cliff-Bear." He explained how Cliff cooked the books to fund and conceal the underground railroad that smuggled drow slaves out of the Underdark. Ty left with a new understanding of his parents and their legacy.

In Session 50, Veil and Freddie plane-shifted the 111 recovered memory crystals to Alistair, who carries them across to the afterlife. It was also revealed that Lolth's curse on Alistair has been falling instead on Roe, who is aging unnaturally as a result. The talisman that breaks the curse is Alistair's old adventuring tricorn, the hat he wore before becoming the ship's captain.
