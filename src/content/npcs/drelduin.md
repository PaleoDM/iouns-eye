Drelduin is a dragon lord who ruled the Emerald Crescent, enslaving the desert tribes under his dominion. He sought the Devil's Oyster fungus for his own purposes. He was defeated by the party in 1088 TA, freeing the Tanner and Dweller factions from his tyranny.
