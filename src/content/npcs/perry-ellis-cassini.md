Co-leader of the Cassini Brothers crime family alongside his brother Oleg. Together they projected a short-range necrotic aura that prevented healing for anyone caught inside it.

In Session 50, Perry Ellis was killed at the Rum Runner Aquifer during the party's raid on the Cassini Brothers' attempt to smuggle 111 memory crystals to Haji. He was the first of the brothers to fall in the engagement.
