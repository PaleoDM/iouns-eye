Young man from the De la Alma family who was captured and subjected to Dr. Sprocket's body augmentation experiments. Now tall and broad with a heavily augmented bionic body, but catatonic and traumatized. His memories were extracted by Sprocket and are tied to sensory triggers — the smell of pulque and rising dough. Rescued by the party (Session 19) and returned to his family's care for rehabilitation.
