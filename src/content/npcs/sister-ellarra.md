Spiritual leader of the Ashen Chapel in the Sparkworks District. Resurrected Thorn using the donated scroll after carefully interrogating the party (Session 3). Provides shelter and spiritual guidance to the resistance. Uses Zone of Truth to verify loyalty. The chapel's catacombs contain one of three known tunnel entrances.

In Session 50, the Cassini Brothers' diversionary force blew the chapel doors and pushed past her into the tunnels. Standing in the wreckage with a shield, she had stepped aside rather than die in numbers she couldn't match. When the party arrived she thanked Ty for showing up and blessed him with Shield of Faith before they descended. In the aftermath of the finale, she and Ghost found that they were rather more interested in each other than in Ty's awkward attempts at a polycule.
