Infant daughter of Meris and Dalrick Caldrow. Born an Aasimar — extremely rare in Ekkorai — with a strange birthmark on her neck and cheek. She reacts unpredictably to divine magic. Vessan intended to use her as a vessel to rupture divine energy across the land. The Celestial Seed cult considers her "irreplaceable" for their Convergence ritual. She is the true target of Sallow's march on Ishetar.
