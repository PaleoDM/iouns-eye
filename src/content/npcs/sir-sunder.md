Veil's mentor and head of the Order of the Ju-Shi intelligence network. Also known as Sifu Stone. An elderly man who meticulously polishes the dolorous bells that signal incursions. Sent Veil on her original mission and has been investigating the Aletheia Procedure's corruption. Opened the portal to the Shadowfell (Session 37) and led the expedition to the Pallbearer's domain. Shed his own disguises/burdens alongside Ty in Odaire (Session 41), accepted by the mists as having completed his own story.

In Session 48, Sunder was arrested by Vine Ashen Bloom on fabricated charges. He refused to fight, unwilling to raise a hand against any monk of the order, and instead played the role of a doddering old fool who had gone quietly. He stalled the tribunal by invoking an ancestral custom from his grandfather's line requiring every name of his family be read aloud before court could be held. Our heroes arrived as his legal counsel, and Sunder invoked trial by combat. After the fight, Sunder turned the tribunal against Vine and her ties to the Coinwrights, ending her reign as Divine Judge.
