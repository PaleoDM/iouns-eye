Veil's mentor and head of the Order of the Ju-Shi intelligence network. Also known as Sifu Stone. An elderly man who meticulously polishes the dolorous bells that signal incursions. Sent Veil on her original mission and has been investigating the Aletheia Procedure's corruption. Opened the portal to the Shadowfell (Session 37) and led the expedition to the Pallbearer's domain. Shed his own disguises/burdens alongside Ty in Odaire (Session 41), accepted by the mists as having completed his own story.
