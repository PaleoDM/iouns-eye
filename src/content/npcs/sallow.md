Gaunt, quiet druid lieutenant who oversees the TTT extraction groves. A true believer in Tolvane's methods with no apparent guilt. Left in charge of the compound during the Restful Lily parley (Session 27). After the compound's leadership fractured, Sallow departed north with approximately 30 soldiers, ostensibly marching on Ishetar. The real objective, revealed in Session 30, is the kidnapping of Ellie Caldrow for the Celestial Seed's Convergence ritual.

During the Battle of the Gates (Session 32), Thump connected with Sallow psychically through the mycelium of the earth via Locate Plants. Sallow revealed himself as a nature supremacist who would see all civilization torn down for nature to reclaim it. Thump lectured him on druidic balance. Sallow was livid. He arrived on the battlefield piloting a juggernaut, a hybrid between an awakened tree and a mechanical war machine, but Hrothgar lured it into a dwarven pit trap.

In Session 33, the juggernaut itself turned against Sallow after Thump persuaded it to stand down, revealing that its power core was a seed stolen from the Myreshade and used to grow it twisted. The juggernaut expelled Sallow from its own control. Sallow now awaits the party at the juggernaut's command center.
