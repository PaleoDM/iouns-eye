Fae-Ethani is the leader of the Dweller faction in the Emerald Crescent and a cousin of Ollithian (Ollie). Hardened by a century of survival under Drelduin's tyranny, she leads her people with fierce determination. After the dragon's defeat, she works to reconcile with the Tanners.
