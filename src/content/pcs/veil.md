## Background
Veil is a monk of the Order of the Ju-Shi, sent on a mission by her mentor Stone-Whisper-Bloom of Sunder (Sir Sunder). She replaced one of the names on a list with her own to infiltrate the Sparkworks meeting.

## Key Moments
- First to cross the underground chasm in the Sparkworks tunnels (Session 6)
- Established a Tea House as an intelligence-gathering bastion facility
- Named "Tempest of Truth" in a formal naming ceremony (Session 37)
- Received the Belt of the Shi-Ho during her naming ceremony
- Built the Veiled Eye spy network to counter rakshasa operations
- Has a missing sister named Gideon who turned to necromancy

- Grappled an unconscious Ty out of the Cassini Brothers' necrotic aura mid-fight, saving him from a death spiral (Session 50)
- Took on the work of returning the 111 recovered memory crystals to their owners after the finale; plane-shifted the lot of them with Freddie to Captain Alistair to carry across (Session 50)
- Began working alongside Sunder to root out remaining corruption inside the Order of the Ju-Shi (post Session 50)

## Character Arc
Veil's arc centers on truth and identity — becoming the Tempest of Truth, building her own intelligence network, and confronting the corruption of the Aletheia Procedure justice system. After the finale, she leaned into the truth part of her name in earnest — investigating ownership of the recovered souls, returning them to their families, and rooting out remaining Coinwright corruption inside the Order of the Ju-Shi.
