## Background
Thump is Ishetar's resident druid, a dutiful halfling who paints the autumn colors on the town's leaves each year. Children leave biscuits out for "the painter." He maintains a deep connection to the natural world and is profoundly affected by the Myreshade Forest's corruption.

## Key Moments
- Guided the party through the Red Vale with expert pathfinding (Session 5)
- Root-linked with the awakened tree Ambrosia, enabling fast-travel between Ishetar and the Restful Lily
- Taught Mickell Caldrow elemental magic after discovering the boy's earth-sense connection
- Cast spike growth in the hell hound den, turning the tide of a deadly fight (Session 3)
- Deeply disturbed by the Myreshade Forest's slow death from TTT extraction magic

## Relationships
- Root-linked to Ambrosia at the Restful Lily
- Teaches Mickell Caldrow elemental magic
