Gwenevere, known as Gwen, is an Eladrin sorcerer from the Feywild seeking to return home. Her Wild Magic is unstable and unpredictable. As a color-shifting fey, her appearance changes with her emotional state. Her portal network becomes a target during the campaign when the fey entity Vespa attempts to exploit it.
