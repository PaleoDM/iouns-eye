## Background
Freddie — full name Wilfredo — is Arden's brother, an Aasimar cleric of Erathis. He joined the party at the beholder's lair (Session 33).

## Key Moments
- Flashback revealed a clash with Arden over their different paths (Session 33)
- Established Skyward Industries as the party's commercial enterprise in Sparkworks (Session 37)
- Manages the business side of the party's operations
- Negotiated with the Quartermaster on the Dancing Dirge, earning a future favor (Session 42)

## Relationships
- Brother to Arden; their relationship involves tension over Arden's past choices
- Cleric of Erathis (the Lawbearer), focused on building civilization and commerce
