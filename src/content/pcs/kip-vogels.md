Kip is a compassionate gnome druid who runs a sanctuary called the Hearth, a sacred space for animals with a planar barrier and portal to the Feywild. He has a strong connection to nature and divine magic, developing a growing relationship with Glittergold over the course of the campaign. Kip authored the in-character narrative "From Hearth to Home," documenting the party's journey through Kalari. During the campaign, he develops the ability to hold animal forms for extended periods.
