## Background
Lyra is a wood elven cleric of the Dawnfather who arrived in Ishetar with her dire badger companion Sprout, pulling a tea cart. She listens to the townsfolk's troubles and offers gentle counsel.

## Key Moments
- Destroyed all imps and lemures with a single channel divinity in the first combat (Session 1)
- Conducted Greater Restoration ritual for Th0l's companion Joy at the Restful Lily (Session 12)
- Heavily involved in diplomatic efforts throughout the campaign
- Created tea stations throughout Ishetar as part of her bastion

## Relationships
- Close with Keeper Vespa at the lighthouse
- Companion: Sprout (dire badger, community mascot and tea steward)
- Mentor figure to the vegepygmy children integrated into Ishetar
