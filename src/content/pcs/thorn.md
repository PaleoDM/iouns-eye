## Background
Thorn is a displaced tribal warrior connected to the natural world. He received a mysterious envelope while tending an overgrown garden, connecting to the root and mycelium superhighway.

## Key Moments
- Died in Session 2 and was resurrected by Sister Ellarra using a donated scroll (Session 3)
- Received the Cap of Mycelium from the Great Self, granting the ability to speak with eukaryotes (Session 7)
- Became ambassador to the fungal colony and protector of the World Trees
- Received the Lore-Axe from Rosie, dryad spirit of the Tree of Ambition (Session 36)
- Displacement by Silverweave Ventures was revealed in Session 33 flashback

- Refused death three separate times in the Rum Runner Aquifer fight via Relentless Rage and Rage Beyond Death (Session 50)

## Character Arc
Thorn's journey parallels the displaced peoples he encounters — the myconids, the RELICs, the kobolds. He becomes a guardian of nature against commodification, committing to protect the seven World Trees. After the finale, he stayed in Sparkworks and turned to rooftop gardening, teaching the industrious labor-focused locals how to grow their own food. Arden has spread the word that Thorn "lectures" on underground horticulture, and he is now often observed by onlookers from other districts. He rarely speaks. The lessons are passed on all the same.
