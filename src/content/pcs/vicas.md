## Background
Vicas is an older green dragonborn and Ishetar's primary physician — not the priestly kind, but the bandage-and-bone-saw kind. He mourns his deceased wife Shallah.

## Key Moments
- Led the complex ritual to save Quetzi the couatl (Session 4)
- Trained militia at the barracks alongside Old Hrothgar
- Consistently served as the party's frontline fighter despite being a healer
- Dragged a hell hound back through Thump's spike growth for maximum damage (Session 3)

## Relationships
- Widower of Shallah
- Trained by Old Hrothgar Earthenstone
- Works closely with Lyra on healing and divine matters
