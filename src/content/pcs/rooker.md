## Background
Rooker is an old curmudgeon from Ishetar's mage academy. He had an eldritch parasite removed in prior adventures and now draws on an unknown internal magic source. He serves as the party's primary strategist and researcher.

## Key Moments
- Introduced in a heated argument with Sombra over an overdue library book (Session 1)
- Placed a crucial Darkness spell during the ogre fight (Session 2)
- Established a library and teleportation circle in Ishetar as bastion facilities
- Discovered Veraine's hidden box containing blood harvesting apparatus (Session 29)
- Sealed the vegepygmy citizenship oath with Elder Sprigg via magical brand (Session 6)

## Relationships
- Works with Sombra (tabaxi librarian and research partner)
- Mentors Meris Caldrow as an apprentice in magical studies
- Former connection to the Red Mantle Academy in Tormellin
