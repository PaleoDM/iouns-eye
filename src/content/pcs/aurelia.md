## Background

Aurelia is a Half-Elf Rogue of the Galanodel family from Laenora. She operates publicly as an exotic goods merchant, concealing her true vocation as a Soul Reaper and Assassin.

She is searching for her vanished grandfather and a mysterious blue smoke-filled glass orb connected to his disappearance.

## Distinguishing Features

Aurelia records each of her kills as mandala tattoos on her body, centered around a swan symbol.
