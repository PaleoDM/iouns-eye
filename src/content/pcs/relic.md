Relic is a soul-bearing construct fighter originally discovered in a magical pod. Created by Arianna Wintar through Project Anima, Relic gains self-awareness and agency throughout the campaign. At the campaign's end, Relic agrees to watch over the other constructs remaining in Kalari.
