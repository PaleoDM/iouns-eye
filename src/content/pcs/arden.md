## Background
Arden is a charismatic bard who served as crew on an airship, wearing shiny armor and a winning smile. He formerly served under Seressa Vale and carries guilt about her death.

## Key Moments
- Gave away his magical returning trident to an old farmer leading the resistance (Session 3)
- Served as First Mate on the Brass Wing during the Roc Run (Sessions 15-17)
- Died to a beholder's death ray and was resurrected by Ty (Session 32)
- Reunited with his brother Freddie/Wilfredo (Session 33)
- Developed a romance with Jerusha, a Silverweave legionnaire (Session 35)

## Relationships
- Brother to Freddie (Wilfredo), an Aasimar cleric
- Romance with Jerusha (Session 35+)
