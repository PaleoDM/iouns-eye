## Structure

The Astorian calendar consists of **14 months** of **28 days** each, totaling **392 days per year**. Each month is named for one of the fourteen Prime Deities, and each deity's holy day falls within their namesake month. Weeks are 7 days long (Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday), giving each month exactly 4 weeks.

## Seasons and Months

### Spring (Months 1–3)
1. **Avandra** — The Changebringer's month. Holy Day: New Year's Day (1st).
2. **Melora** — The Wildmother's month. Holy Day: Wild's Grandeur (14th).
3. **Yondalla** — The Luck Spirit's month. Holy Day: Luck's Favor (21st).

### Summer (Months 4–7)
4. **Corellon** — The Archheart's month. Holy Day: Elvendawn (21st).
5. **Ioun** — The Knowing Mistress's month. Holy Day: The Knowing (14th).
6. **Pelor** — The Dawnfather's month. Holy Day: Highsun (21st).
7. **Glittergold** — The GoldSoul's month. Holy Day: Fizzleboom (14th).

### Autumn (Months 8–10)
8. **Moradin** — The All-Hammer's month. Holy Day: Deep Solace (18th).
9. **Erathis** — The Lawbearer's month. Holy Day: Harvest Throne (22nd).
10. **Obitris** — The Gate Keeper's month. Holy Day: Gatekeeper's Remembrance (5th).

### Winter (Months 11–14)
11. **Sehanine** — The Moonweaver's month. Holy Day: Moonweaver's Masquerade (21st).
12. **Kord** — The Stormlord's month. Holy Day: Day of Challenging (7th).
13. **Bahamut** — The Platinum Dragon's month. Holy Day: Embertide (5th).
14. **Raei** — The Everlight's month. Holy Day: Day of Forgiveness (14th).

## Dates and Notation

Dates are written as **day month-name year** — for example, **25 Bahamut 1136**. The year designation "TA" (Third Age) is used in display but omitted in raw data. The calendar's month order is fixed and canonical — it does not change from year to year.

## Relationship to the Moons

The 28-day month is synchronized with the lunar cycle of the Celestial Braid. The 7th of each month is the New Moon, and the 21st is the Full Moon, with King Tides and Fool Tides following the same monthly pattern.
