## Description

Shadow Rifthaven is the Shadowfell reflection of the city of Rifthaven -- a wicked place of terrible darkness and sharp angles, haunted by specters and wraiths. The party glimpsed this shadow echo briefly as they fell through the planes after Sir Sunder opened a portal to pursue the Pallbearer. They did not linger, as their plane shift carried them onward into the mist-shrouded Domain of Dread known as Odaire. A dark talisman attuned to no particular domain would be needed to arrive specifically in Shadow Rifthaven rather than a Domain of Dread.
