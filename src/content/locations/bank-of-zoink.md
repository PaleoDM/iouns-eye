A massive underground marble hall accessed via the BDZ teleporting scale. Features an enormous marble wall fitted with rows and columns of spokes, with giant gold rings bouncing along them like a colossal abacus. Operated by a tiny, ancient golden dragonborn. The name "Zoink" derives from Bonqka dhiz Zoink, an ancient draconic phrase inscribed on the teleporting scale. The party arrived here after extracting the Harsk family in Session 31.
