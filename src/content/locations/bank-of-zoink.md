A massive underground marble hall accessed via the BDZ teleporting scale. Features an enormous marble wall fitted with rows and columns of spokes, with giant gold rings bouncing along them like a colossal abacus. Operated by a tiny, ancient golden dragonborn named Zoink. The name derives from Bonqka dhiz Zoink, an ancient draconic phrase inscribed on the teleporting scale. The party arrived here after extracting the Harsk family in Session 31.

The bank operates as a demiplane, a neutral magical marketplace where powerful factions, governments, and individuals across all of Astoria can trade goods and services without physically moving them. Account holders use access cards and passcodes. The TTT maintained an account here with several hundred thousand gold. The bank offers alert insurance (notifying holders when funds move) and exclusivity protections (restricting who can access an account), sold separately. Zoink also offers teleportation services for a fee.
