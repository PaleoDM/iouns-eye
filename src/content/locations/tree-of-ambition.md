## Description

The Tree of Ambition is an ancient crystalline tree located in the Boughshadow highlands above Rifthaven. It is a short but massive specimen composed of a quartz trunk, gypsum leaves, opal flowers, and basaltic roots that extend deep into the mountain. The tree is guarded by a treant named Rosie, one of several guardians entrusted to protect trees cut by the original gardeners in a time long forgotten.

Silverweave Ventures sought to commodify the tree, deploying legionnaires and an anti-ward apparatus to breach Nanny Pu'Pu's protective wards. The party, with assistance from the green dragon Scourge, defeated the Silverweave forces and used the Whisper-Wings to magically compel the surviving agents to report the tree as lost. Thorn learned that two of the ancient trees had been compromised: his own and one other.
