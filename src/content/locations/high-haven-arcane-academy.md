A major arcane institution in Rifthaven. One of two organizations (alongside the Order of the Ju Shi) with the constitutional authority to invoke a vote of no confidence in the Aletheia Procedure, triggering a governmental review and operational freeze. Gideon, Veil's brother, holds a department chair in the school of necromancy here.
