A major arcane institution in Rifthaven. One of two organizations (alongside the Order of the Ju Shi) with the constitutional authority to invoke a vote of no confidence in the Aletheia Procedure, triggering a governmental review and operational freeze. Gideon, Veil's brother, holds a department chair in the school of necromancy here.

The school of necromancy is well-funded and actively markets the civic applications of the dark arts, including messaging that "healing should be considered necromancy." Professor Serrano, an emeritus gnome who pioneered ghoul extraction, works here. Bilver Shadowgift's partially reconstituted soul was kept in a chrysalis tank in the lower levels.
