## Description

Glennox is one of the three known continents of Astoria. It contains two major regions that have featured in play: Kalari and the Crucible.

## Known Regions

- **Kalari** — Region within Glennox, setting of the Ruins of Kalari campaign (1088–1089 TA).
- **The Crucible** — Region within Glennox.
