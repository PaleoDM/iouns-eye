## Description

The Glamour District is Rifthaven's entertainment and nightlife hub, reminiscent of New Orleans' French Quarter. It is renowned for its vibrant late-night scene featuring theaters, opera houses, nightclubs, and bars. The district houses the Serenity Auction House and the Gilded Ember gambling hall, which was run by the cult leader Vandro Morrix before his death during the Sparkworks liberation. The Cassini Brothers also operate gambling operations in the district.
