## Description

The Hearthfire Commons is an old tenement building in the southwest side of the Sparkworks District. During the Hushed Choir's occupation, the cultists retreated to the Hearthfire Commons after losing ground to the growing civil resistance led by Sister Ellarra's Ashen Chapel and Lord Harwin's manor. The party eventually planned and executed an assault on the Hearthfire Commons to deliver a killing blow to the cult's remaining forces, defeating dozens of cultists and breaking the Choir's hold on the district for good.
