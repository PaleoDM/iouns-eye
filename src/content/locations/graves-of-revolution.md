## Description

The Graves of the Revolution is a monument in the Sparkworks District commemorating those who fell in a previous uprising. Beneath the monument lies one of the three known entrances into the Sparkworks Tunnels, alongside the Ashen Chapel's catacombs and Lord Harwin's manor cellars. The memorial stands as a reminder of the district's long history of resistance against oppression.
