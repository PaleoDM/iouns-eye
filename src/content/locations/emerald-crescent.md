The Emerald Crescent is a desert region in Kalari ruled by the dragon Drelduin until his defeat in 1088 TA. It contains the settlements of two rival factions: the Tanners, led by Harlan, and the Dwellers, led by Fae-Ethani. After Drelduin's defeat, the two factions work toward reconciliation.
