## Description

Odaire is a Domain of Dread in the Shadowfell, ruled by Maligno -- a sentient carrionette who desperately wishes he had been made properly real. The domain is a twisted puppet kingdom that traps children, who arrive in an orphanage under the oppressive watch of the Warden. They age only as their own perception of maturity changes, and many wander Odaire for years without ever growing up.

The domain contains Giuseppe's Toy Shop, where Maligno's guilt-ridden creator lives in torment, and is watched over by the Faerie Godmother, a celestial ally of Lulu's who works to preserve hope. The party entered Odaire after a Shadar-kai assassination attempt forced them through a portal into the Shadowfell. They defeated Maligno, rescued the Birthday Host (later named Turmoil), and recovered a calling card that would allow passage to the Pallbearer's domain -- Potter's Field.
