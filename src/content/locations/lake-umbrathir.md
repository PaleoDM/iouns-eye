## Description

Lake Umbrathir is a vast underground lake adjacent to the drow city of Tyr'Zareth beneath Rifthaven. Its rocky beaches and open waters are illuminated by the gentle light of the fourth moon, which reflects across its surface. The clergy of the Moonweaver's temple mine and refine Selenualite -- also known as 4th Moon Pearl -- from the lake's shores, a tightly regulated mercury-like substance that shifts between liquid and solid at room temperature. The discovery of Selenualite far from Lake Umbrathir was a key clue in unraveling a conspiracy at the Temple of the Moonweaver.
