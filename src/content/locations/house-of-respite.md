## Description

The House of Respite is a rundown inn in the Sparkworks District, operated by the Stoutwater family. When the party first arrived, the establishment had seen much better days -- cobwebs lined the corners and the house was in bad shape. The Stoutwaters, led by matriarch Maribel Stoutwater, were doing their best to maintain a sense of normalcy despite having had no real customers in months or even years. The party stayed here on their first night in Sparkworks, and Maribel provided them with a map of the district.
