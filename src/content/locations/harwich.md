Harwich is a cursed village in the Kalari region dealing with pseudo-vampirism among its inhabitants. The curse is tied to the Harwich noble family, particularly Vanir Harwich, and has transformed his lover Celia Varynna into a Dryad. Father Templeton works tirelessly to end the affliction.
