## Description

The Brazier is a working-class area in the northwest side of the Sparkworks District, known for its gambling den and fighting pit. During the Hushed Choir's control of Sparkworks, the cult operated out of the Brazier as their primary base of operations. The area's seedy establishments provided cover for the cult's activities before the party and the citizens of Sparkworks drove them out during the liberation.
