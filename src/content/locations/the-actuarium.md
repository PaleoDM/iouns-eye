A hall of ledgers in the Potter's Field where every soul that enters or leaves the domain is cataloged. Bureaucratic and meticulous, it is exactly the kind of place where evidence lives. The party visited the Actuarium in Session 43 to search for records on Quin's soul, where they encountered and killed Divine Judge Josephine Maren. The ledger she was holding confirmed Quin's soul was held in the Pallbearer's Galleria.
