## Description

Potter's Field is a Domain of Dread in the Shadowfell ruled by the Pallbearer, operating as a capitalist afterlife where souls are traded as currency. The domain manifests as a white marble city with docks, a customs house, and an Atrium where souls are processed and judged. Visitors must declare their magic items and offer collateral -- cherished memories, gold, luck, or similar personal currency -- to enter.

The party arrived aboard the ghost ship Dancing Dirge, captained by the spectral pirate Alistair (revealed as Tyrell's biological father), after coating themselves in leviathan blood to mask their living auras. Inside, they confronted Divine Judge Josephine Maren, one of the seven High Justicars, and discovered that Quin's soul is held in the Pallbearer's personal collection known as the Galleria. The campaign's current arc involves planning a heist to recover it.
