## Description

The Sparkworks District is an impoverished, partially underground district of Rifthaven where the Rifthaven Online campaign begins. Once a thriving industrial area with Victorian-style homes built into cliff faces, it had fallen into severe disrepair by 1128 TA, with most buildings in terrible condition and virtually no commerce. The district was oppressed by the Hushed Choir cult and the corrupt Ledgermen militia until the party liberated it during the Sparkworks Revolution.

Following the liberation, the district underwent significant rebuilding. The party established bastions in the former manors, recruited defenders, and revitalized the community. Sparkworks became the party's home base for the remainder of the campaign, eventually hosting barracks, workshops, an armory, temples, and even an elk farm.
