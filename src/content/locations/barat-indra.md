Barat-Indra is an active volcano in the Ekkorai region of Wanun. It contains a temple to forgotten gods and murals depicting Kyzdurat imprisoning a primordial djinn. At its caldera lies a portal to the Fire Plane, through which Zashien fell, ending the Head Hunters campaign.
