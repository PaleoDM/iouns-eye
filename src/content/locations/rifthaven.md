## Description

Rifthaven is an ancient city on the continent of Khanae, dating to the dawn of civilization. Built near the mysterious Rift, it is surrounded by dense jungle and features dramatically varied districts spanning multiple elevation levels. The city is home to diverse factions including the Order of the Ju-Shi, the Syndicate, and various criminal organizations.

## Districts

- **Bloomridge** — Known for breathtaking fields of vibrant and magical flowers
- **Boughshadow** — High in the peaks, guards against jungle dangers, home to druid community
- **Glamour** — Entertainment and nightlife, resembles a fantasy French Quarter
- **Gloomhaven** — Shrouded in mystery
- **High Haven** — Education hub, home to High Haven Academy
- **Honeywine** — Famous for honey and mead production
- **Silkspun** — Tabaxi community, contains Nightmarket
- **Sporestone** — Old milling district, home to the Mint
- **Psycoral** — Residential, contains abandoned tenements
- **Tradeheart** — Commercial hub, home to Grand Souk
