## Description

Nanny Pu'Pu's Hut is the dwelling of the ancient hag Nanny Pu'Pu in the Boughshadow highlands. The entrance is marked by a gate wrought of bone crowned with a minotaur skull knocker. Her garden is strange and unsettling, filled with disturbed graves and composting mushrooms. On her porch, the party found three pairs of Silverweave legionnaire boots -- the remains of soldiers she claimed to have eaten.

The hag has lived in Boughshadow for centuries, maintaining protective wards over the area and the Tree of Ambition. She is known for her ability to make people forget what haunts them or remember what they have repressed. In exchange for revealing the truth about Quin's memories, she demanded a dragon egg from the caverns below, a task that deliberately put the party in the path of the beholder Ozythar.
