## Description

Tyr'Zareth is an Underdark district of Rifthaven, home to the drow and the lakeshore haven where Tyrell was raised. The city is lit by soft phosphorescent glow and features manicured cavern gardens. Tyrell's family estate sits within the district, where his parents Cliff and Roe reside alongside his sister Phoebe.

The district is also home to the Hall of Reflection, the Temple of the Moonweaver, where the clergy mines and refines Selenualite (4th Moon Pearl) from the shores of nearby Lake Umbrathir. The temple was the site of a conspiracy involving ritualistic break-ins and the infiltration of the acolyte Breccia, which the party helped expose during a personal interlude.
