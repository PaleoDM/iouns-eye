Fortified logging operation and military installation south of Ishetar. Houses approximately 200 people — mostly laborers and families — with 25-30 combatants. Protected by magical thorn walls, spike growth perimeters, and entangling root networks. Contains workshops, barracks, family quarters, extraction groves, and a hidden cellar where blood harvesting was conducted. Leadership fractured after the party's assault in Session 29.
