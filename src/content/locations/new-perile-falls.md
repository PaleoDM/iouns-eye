New Perile Falls is a city in the Kalari region of Glennox, founded approximately fifty years before the campaign by Papa Luhar Perile. Built to replace Old Perile Falls after its catastrophic collapse, the city has grown into a modern center of commerce and governance. It is governed by Mayor Cecil Perile and a council of oligarchs, with Constable Karash maintaining order.
