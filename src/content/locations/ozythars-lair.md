## Description

Ozythar's Lair is the underground domain of the beholder Ozythar the Tyrant, located deep within the Boughshadow Caverns. Access requires descending a sheer vertical shaft into a jagged, multi-island lair patrolled by Gauth sentries and a massive Froghemoth. The lair is defended by Ozythar's anti-magic cone and eyestalk rays, making enchanted flight unreliable.

The party rappelled into the lair using rope and pitons rather than risk magical flight. The ensuing battle was brutal -- Quin was disintegrated by one of Ozythar's eye rays, and Arden fell unconscious. Tyrell delivered the killing blow with a devastating smite. The hoard contained some 26,000 gold pieces along with magical items including a rune of stealth, a rune of glamour, and a double-bladed glaive-halberd.
