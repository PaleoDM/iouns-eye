## Description

The Tradeheart District is the commercial hub of Rifthaven, bustling with merchants, colorful stalls, and unique goods. It is home to the Grand Souk, the city's large marketplace featuring performers and merchants. The district serves as the economic heart of Rifthaven's trade operations.
