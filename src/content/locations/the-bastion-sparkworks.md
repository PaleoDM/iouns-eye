## Description

The Bastion is the party's headquarters in Sparkworks, originally Lord Harwin Brassfell's brother's manor. Harwin gifted the estate to the party after they liberated the district from the Hushed Choir. Over the course of the campaign, the party expanded it extensively with a barracks for recruited defenders, an armory, a smithy, workshops, a garden, a library, an arcane study, a sanctuary, a scriptorium, a trophy room, a sacristy, and even stables housing an elk farm. Captain Reeve serves as the minister of defense, overseeing the Bastion's military operations.
