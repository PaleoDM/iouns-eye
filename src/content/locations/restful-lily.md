A Victorian manor house turned sacred sanctuary and spa in the Myreshade Forest. Co-founded by the elven sisters Sylvari and Solara after the party broke their curses (medusa and lamia respectively). Features hot springs with Greater Restoration magic guarded by the water nymph Cyrena, gardens defended by the awakened tree Ambrosia, and serves as a diplomatic neutral ground. The Restful Lily Parley between the party and the TTT took place here (Session 27).
