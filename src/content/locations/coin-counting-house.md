A building in the Hustler's District used by the Cassini Brothers as a base of operations. In Session 49 the party staked it out using Freddie's arcane eye, discovered the trap set for them, and bypassed it entirely by striking the second wave ambush outside. Freddie then used Major Dunthar's forged signet ring orders to have the thugs inside clear out the building's contents and deliver everything to the party's safe house.
