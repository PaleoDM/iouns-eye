## Description

The Boughshadow Caverns are an extensive underground system beneath the Boughshadow highlands, accessed through a cracked well in the old ruins above. The caverns are infested with kobolds loyal to a dragon cult and Kruthik creatures that serve the beholder Ozythar. Thieves' Cant markings within the tunnels point toward the local witch Nanny Pu'Pu.

The party navigated the cavern system in a prolonged dungeon crawl, brokering alliances with kobold factions and clashing with Silverweave Ventures legionnaires led by Officer Oswald who had established a camp within. The caverns also house the lair of the green dragon Scourge, who allied with the party after they defeated Ozythar.
