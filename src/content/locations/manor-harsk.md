The Harsk family estate in the foothills of the Astral Peaks. A stone-walled manor house on a hillside with stables, servant passages, and surrounding fields where the family grazed their feldunost herd. Staffed for over two centuries by Udo and Bessa. Seized by Callista Vohn under Guildmaster orders in Session 31. Set ablaze by Lady Isla Harsk during the family's extraction rather than let it fall into enemy hands.
