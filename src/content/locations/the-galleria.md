The Pallbearer's private art collection, housed in a grand estate within the Potter's Field. The public-facing galleries catalog the dead with clinical precision. The real collection — the serious inventory — is in a cellar below the main floor, accessible only through the estate proper. Souls are held here as painted portraits: collected, cataloged, and kept.

The party heisted the Galleria in Session 44, recovering the souls of Quin and Divine Judge Art Doyle. Veil also found an empty portrait frame bearing her brother Gideon's name, with a reserved spot already paid for. She took the frame with her on the way out.

The estate is managed by the major domo Doragnes and staffed by the Paintress, a blind artist who works in an upper gallery and appears to serve a recruitment function for the Pallbearer.
