A monastic outpost and military stronghold in the waters between Ishetar and Emberspost. Safeguards knowledge of "the Rift" — a dangerous region. When the party arrived (Session 23), the island was under siege by an undead army emerging from the sea. The abbot was dead and the monastery was falling. The undead assault is connected to High Prince Brof's deal with the demon lord Syrgaul.
