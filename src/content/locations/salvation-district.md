## Description

The Salvation District is a mountainous agricultural zone in Rifthaven, known for its barley crops, leathery cactus fruit, and animal husbandry. The district was controlled by the Sergente family, who ran a plantation operation backed by the House of Coinwrights. Under Lord Sergente's rule, laborers were oppressed and economic tensions ran high, with growing discontent among the populace.

The party allied with the de la Alma family to overthrow the Sergente regime, fighting through the district's streets, assaulting the Moving Convoy fortress, and ultimately infiltrating the plantation manor. The liberation of Salvation exposed Dr. Sprocket's body augmentation experiments and dealt a significant blow to the Rakshasa Haji's schemes to secure infernal financing.
