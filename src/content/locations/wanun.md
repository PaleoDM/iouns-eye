## Description

Wanun is one of the three known continents of Astoria. It is the setting for the Ishetar campaigns, with the region of Ekkorai serving as the primary adventuring area.

## Known Regions

- **Ekkorai** — The region within Wanun where the Ishetar campaigns take place.
