## Description

The Ashen Chapel is a house of worship in the Sparkworks District, tended by Sister Ellarra. It served as one of two bases of civil disobedience during the Sparkworks uprising, alongside Lord Harwin's manor. The chapel contains catacombs that connect to one of the three known entrances into the Sparkworks Tunnels beneath the district. During the revolution, Sister Ellarra used the chapel to shelter and heal the wounded, including reviving Thorn after he fell in an early street battle.

In Session 50, the Cassini Brothers' diversionary force blew the chapel doors and pushed past Sister Ellarra into the tunnels en route to the Rum Runner Aquifer.
