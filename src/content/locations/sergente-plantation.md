## Description

The Sergente Plantation is the wealthy estate of Lord Maximilian Sergente in the Salvation District, serving as the operational center of the Coinwrights' foothold in the area. The compound includes the plantation manor, a secondary building housing an Arcane Forge used for Dr. Sprocket's body augmentation experiments, and two barracks for guards and Ledgermen.

The party infiltrated the plantation through old servants' passages while Luz de la Alma and her crew provided a distraction by assaulting the walls with Dendritium Augers. Inside, they destroyed the Arcane Forge -- which the Rakshasa Haji had planned to use as proof of concept for a loan from Mammon -- recovered Captain Reeve's infernal contract, and cornered Lord Sergente in his panic room. Sergente was captured and handed over to the people of Salvation for sentencing.
