Old Perile Falls is the ancient capital city of the Kalari region that fell catastrophically approximately two hundred years before the campaign (~888 TA). A storm drake attack triggered the city's collapse into a sinkhole, leaving it as underwater ruins. Senate Speaker Runva Doberdower led the evacuation while Byron Wintar sacrificed himself to keep the arch-gate open. The ruins remain a dangerous site of exploration, holding secrets of Arianna Wintar's Project Anima.
