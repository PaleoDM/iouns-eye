## Description

Boughshadow is a highland region in the peaks above Rifthaven, once a full district that guarded the city against the dangers of the jungle. The district died off roughly 250 years ago through population decline and had been erased from modern maps by the time the party visited. The area contains old ruins, a cracked well leading to underground caverns, and is home to the hag Nanny Pu'Pu.

The party flew to Boughshadow aboard Captain Vex's Brass Wing to investigate rumors of the old witch and confront threats in the cavern system below. The region is also the site of the Tree of Ambition, a crystalline tree guarded by a treant named Rosie, which Silverweave Ventures attempted to commodify before the party intervened.
