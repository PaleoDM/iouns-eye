An ancient woodland region in Ekkorai that is dying from the Turquoise Timber Trust's extraction magic. Trees appear green outside but are hollow inside; the soil is grey and exhausted. The damage is visible south of the river and spreading northward. Ambrosia can sense the corruption through the root network. Home to the Restful Lily sanctuary.
