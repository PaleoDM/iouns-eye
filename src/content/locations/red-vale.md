A haunted lowland valley in Ekkorai formed by an ancient asteroid impact. The extraterrestrial mineral soil supports unusual life, including the vegepygmy people who originated from the "sky rock." Contains ancient battlefields with oath-bound undead, including the Braided Spear where Kareth Varn's relics were scattered. Serves as the trade route between Ishetar and the vegepygmy settlement.
