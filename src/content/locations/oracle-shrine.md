An old, forgotten shrine north of Ishetar housing a "well of divinity" — a source of raw divine magic that Quetzi the couatl was set to guard. The wards keeping the divine energy dormant were never meant to be permanent, and after nearly four centuries they are wearing out. Vessan attempted to weaponize this location using Ellie Caldrow as a vessel. The wards are now failing, leaking wild divinity into the surrounding land.
