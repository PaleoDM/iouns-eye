## Overview

Set 12 years after the original Ishetar campaign, Head Hunters follows a party dispatched to investigate a missing academic expedition in the Fractured Expanse. The campaign ran for 5 sessions before ending when Zashien fell through a portal to the Fire Plane at the caldera of Barat-Indra.

## Major Arcs

### The Missing Expedition
The party investigates MisChief Mining's reopened dig site and discovers the academic expedition led by Dr. Nastase has been raised as undead by Inotus Rex, the Necromancer King. They defeat the undead and explore the Old Founder's Laboratory built by the artificer Ashok.

### The Founder's Legacy
The party learns about the Founders — legendary artificers including Ashok, Alanah, and the fire giant Kyzdurat — who left ruins scattered throughout the Fractured Expanse. They discover Kyzdurat imprisoned a primordial djinn beneath Mount Barat-Indra.

### The Caldera
At the volcanic summit, the party is caught between the Arsons of Anarchy fire cult and the Ornerian villagers, both connected to prophecies about a "man of flame and flesh." Zashien falls into the caldera and passes through a portal to the Fire Plane, effectively ending the campaign.

## Themes

- Exploration of ancient ruins and lost civilizations
- Elemental heritage and prophecy
- The dangers of forbidden knowledge
