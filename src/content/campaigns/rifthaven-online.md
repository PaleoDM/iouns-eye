## Overview

Rifthaven Online follows a group of strangers brought together by a mysterious tiefling named Dante in the impoverished Sparkworks District. What begins as a simple smuggling tunnel job spirals into a district-wide revolution, an airship race, a liberation campaign, a dungeon crawl against a beholder, and ultimately a journey through the Shadowfell's Domains of Dread to recover a companion's stolen soul. The campaign spans from 1128 to 1129 TA across 43 sessions, with the party growing from level 3 to level 11.

## Major Arcs

### Arc 1: Sparkworks Tunnels (Sessions 1–12)
Hired by Dante to investigate smuggling tunnels beneath the Sparkworks District, the party discovers a vast underground network guarded by a fragmented fungal colony (the Great Self). They liberate the district from the Hushed Choir cult and the corrupt Ledgermen militia, earning the community's trust and Lord Harwin's manor as their base of operations—the Bastion.

### Arc 2: Personal Storylines (Sessions 13–14)
Quin infiltrates the Cassini Brothers' vault to recover his stolen memories, encountering the unsettling Dr. Solvane. Tyrell visits the drow city of Tyr'Zareth, discovers he is a reincarnated elf, and uncovers a conspiracy involving the Temple of the Moonweaver.

### Arc 3: The Roc Run (Sessions 15–17)
The party joins Captain Lysa Vex's airship crew for Rifthaven's legendary Roc Run race. They survive yugoloth mercenaries sent by the Rakshasa Haji, win the race, and encounter the mysterious druid organization Earthen Loam at the victory banquet.

### Arc 4: Salvation & Boughshadow (Sessions 18–36)
A two-front arc. In the Salvation District, the party allies with the de la Alma family to overthrow Lord Sergente's Coinwright-backed plantation operation, rescuing RELICs and exposing Dr. Sprocket's body augmentation experiments. In Boughshadow, they navigate a kobold-infested cavern system, defeat the beholder Ozythar (losing Quin to disintegration), ally with the green dragon Scourge, and protect the Tree of Ambition from Silverweave Ventures' commodification plot.

### Arc 5: Shadowfell — Odaire (Sessions 37–41)
A Shadar-kai assassination attempt forces the party through a portal into the Shadowfell. They enter Odaire, a Domain of Dread ruled by Maligno, a sentient carrionette who has trapped dozens of children in an eternal puppet kingdom. The party defeats Maligno, rescues the Birthday Host (later named Turmoil), and escapes with the Pallbearer's calling card.

### Arc 6: Potter's Field (Sessions 42–43, ongoing)
Guided by the ghost ship Dancing Dirge and its captain Alistair (revealed as Tyrell's biological father), the party enters Potter's Field—a Domain of Dread that operates as a capitalist afterlife where souls are traded as currency. They defeat Divine Judge Josephine Maren and locate Quin's soul portrait in the Pallbearer's personal Galleria, setting up a heist to recover it.

## Themes

- Revolution and community building against systemic oppression
- The commodification of labor, bodies, and souls
- Found family versus biological family
- Identity, reincarnation, and who we choose to become
- The price of justice when institutions are corrupt
