## Overview

Rifthaven IRL is the predecessor to Rifthaven Online, set in the ancient city of Rifthaven on the continent of Khanae. The campaign ran for 7 sessions (September–November 2023) before being rebooted as Rifthaven Online with a different party but the same world.

The campaign established extensive world details about Rifthaven's districts, factions, and threats that carry forward into Rifthaven Online.

## Major Arcs

### The Discordant Fellowship
The party, working for the mysterious tiefling patron Dante, investigates the chaos cult known as the Discordant Fellowship. They raid their illegal medallion-minting operation at the Sporestone Mint, rescue the cleric Karina from a cult vault, and stop the cult leader Vorak Bloodfang's attack on the Grand Souk marketplace.

### The Heartwood Blight
Traveling to the Boughshadow district, the party discovers the ancient Heartwood Tree in the Verdelorn Woods has been struck by a shadowfey blight. They navigate dangerous plateaus, fight red caps and shadow creatures, and confront Nighthollow, the Queen of Thorns, in the Shadowfell to remove the corrupting thorn.

### Malgorth the Soulflayer
The demon Malgorth previously possessed Lord Reginald Darkmoor, who was assassinated by the Syndicate operative Seraphina Duskwallow. Dante tasks the party with acquiring Darkmoor's ashes from the Serenity Auction House for a ritual to sever the party's growing demonic connections.

## Themes

- Urban intrigue and factional politics
- Shadow magic corruption and its seductive power
- Ancient evils lurking beneath civilization
