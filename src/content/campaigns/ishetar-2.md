Ishetar 2.0 picks up fifty-four years after the original Ishetar campaign. The colony has grown from a fledgling outpost to a thriving settlement on the verge of independence from the Aroiin Empire. Tensions with the empire are at an all-time high, and the frontier of Ekkorai remains dangerously wild. The campaign follows five new heroes as they defend Ishetar from converging threats: the Turquoise Timber Trust's exploitative logging operation, an undead army mobilizing from the sea, the Celestial Seed cult hunting an Aasimar infant, and the slow fracturing of the Aroiin Empire itself.
