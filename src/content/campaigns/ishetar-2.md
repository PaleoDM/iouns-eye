## Overview

Ishetar 2.0 picks up fifty-four years after the original Ishetar campaign. The colony has grown from a fledgling outpost to a thriving settlement on the verge of independence from the Aroan Empire. Tensions with the empire are at an all-time high, and the frontier of Ekkorai remains dangerously wild. The campaign follows five new heroes as they defend Ishetar from converging threats: the Turquoise Timber Trust's exploitative logging operation, an undead army mobilizing from the sea, the Celestial Seed cult hunting an Aasimar infant, and the slow fracturing of the Aroan Empire itself.

## Major Arcs

### Arc 1: Help the Caldrows (Sessions 1–4)
The party rescues the Caldrow family from Vessan, a twisted celestial being who sought to use the infant Ellie Caldrow as a vessel for wild divine energy. They save the couatl oracle Quetzi and learn that the divine well north of Ishetar is leaking wild magic as its ancient wards fail.

### Arc 2: Deliver Meat to the Vegepygmies (Sessions 5–6)
A trade mission through the dangerous Red Vale leads to a diplomatic breakthrough. The party negotiates an oath-bound agreement with Elder Sprigg, integrating the vegepygmy people as citizens of Ishetar in exchange for winter food production and mutual defense.

### Arc 3: The Price of Beauty (Sessions 9–12)
The party discovers the Restful Lily, a cursed sanctuary deep in the Myreshade Forest. They break the curses on Sylvari (medusa) and Solara (lamia), restoring them and the sanctuary. The Restful Lily becomes a critical safe haven and diplomatic neutral ground, with the awakened tree Ambrosia serving as guardian and root-network sentinel.

### Arc 4: Emberspost (Sessions 13–22)
Called to aid the coastal city of Emberspost, the party uncovers an assassination plot against Duchess Lyla orchestrated by the Seven Eyes through corrupted priest Father Assam and the agent Vicar Xona. Lolth herself intervenes to hang Xona for overreach. The party gains sea passage and political allies.

### Arc 5: Firewatch Island (Sessions 23–25)
En route home, the party stops at Firewatch Island monastery, only to find it under siege by an undead army emerging from the sea. They learn this force is connected to High Prince Brof's deal with the demon lord Syrgaul, and that the Rift holds dangerous knowledge the monastery was meant to guard.

### Arc 6: The Turquoise Timber Trust (Sessions 26–30, ongoing)
Returning to a changed Ishetar, the party confronts the TTT's environmental destruction of the Myreshade Forest. A parley at the Restful Lily exposes Veraine Luthaire as a Celestial Seed agent hunting Ellie Caldrow. The party assaults the TTT compound, fracturing its leadership. Castellan Drev Harsk switches sides, but lieutenant Sallow marches north toward Ishetar with the true objective of kidnapping Ellie for the cult's Convergence ritual.

## Themes

- Colonial independence and self-determination against imperial control
- Environmental stewardship versus industrial exploitation
- The leaking of wild divine magic into a post-divine world
- Found family and community building on the frontier
- Converging conspiracies threatening a fragile peace
