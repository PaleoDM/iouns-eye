## Overview

SKT One Last Job follows a party recruited by the Peregrine Society to investigate giant attacks across the Cradle of Conflict (the northern region of Glennox). The cosmic law known as the Ordning — which governed giant hierarchy — has shattered, allowing five giant lords to vie for supremacy. The party collects magical conches, confronts giant lords, and ultimately prevents the blue dragon Iymrith and the primordial kraken Slarkrethel from ascending to godhood.

## Major Arcs

### The Ordning Shattered
Giant attacks destabilize the region as the ancient hierarchy collapses. The Peregrine Society recruits the party to investigate and contain the crisis.

### Giant Lords Campaign
The party confronts multiple giant lords: Thane Kayalithica of the stone giants, Chief Guuh of the hill giants, and Jarl Storvald of the frost giants. Each lord seeks to prove their clan's supremacy by collecting artifacts of power.

### The Storm King's Court
The party discovers King Hekkathon of the storm giants has been captured. They rescue him and learn the Ordning's shattering was engineered by Iymrith, an ancient blue dragon disguised as a storm giant advisor.

### Iymrith and Slarkrethel
The final arc reveals a dual threat: Iymrith seeks to seize storm giant power while the primordial kraken Slarkrethel manipulates events from the deep. The party defeats both entities, restoring order to the giant hierarchy.

## Themes

- The collapse of ancient hierarchies and power vacuums
- Small folk standing against titanic forces
- Hidden manipulation and disguised enemies
- Duty and one last mission before retirement
