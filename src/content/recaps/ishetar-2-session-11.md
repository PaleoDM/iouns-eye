We opened with our heroes waking in the Restful Lily, preparing to investigate what they strongly suspected to be a hag coven hidden within the spa. In the morning, the party split up. Thump and Lyra attended a sun salutation in the gardens, led by the elven woman known as Azirsa. While taking part, Lyra realized that Azirsa was quietly draining vitality from the guests through the exercise. Afterward, Thump was invited to compete against the **Sun Tree**, a cutting said to descend from the original trees planted by the Dawnfather. Thump passed each test—strength, agility, and wisdom—and impressed the tree, who promised him a cutting if he could help restore the two original sisters who once owned the estate.

Meanwhile, Vicas and Azrael visited the bathhouse, where they met **Bartholomew (Th0l)**, a drow adventurer from the Peregrine Society. His memories seemed clouded, and guilt weighed heavily on him—his partner had disappeared, but the details slipped away from him. As they spoke, the bath’s resident spirit, a nymph in the waters, whispered directly into their ears. She warned that the three elf “sisters” who owned the spa were in truth hags, and that the true twin sisters who founded the Lily were still present somewhere on the grounds.

Rooker, meanwhile, mingled with other guests and probed at the Lily’s magic. He concluded the sisters were feeding on insecurities, preying upon the doubts of their visitors and harvesting vitality to maintain their own beauty. He also suspected the magical portraits in the spa played a key role.

When the group reconvened to share their findings, they quickly noticed that **Sombra was missing**.

The search led them to follow tracks toward either the invisible tower or the old shrine. Choosing the shrine, they broke in through shattered glass and found signs of desecration—broken mirrors and a faded portrait of two elf sisters, one golden, the other silver. From below, they heard weeping. Descending into the cellar, they discovered the sisters in corrupted forms: one as a **Medusa**, the other as a **Lamia**.

Combat erupted in the close quarters. Azrael was nearly turned to stone when he locked eyes with the Medusa. ~~Thinking quickly, Thump retrieved the portrait, and with Lyra’s *Dispel Magic* they destroyed it, unleashing the Dawnfather’s radiance to weaken the sisters’ fury.~~

> DM Correction: Thump did dispel magic and Lyra destroyed it!

The fight turned, and Azrael managed to resist petrification long enough to break free. Slowly, the Medusa and Lamia’s venom and rage burned away.

As the battle ended, the two sisters’ forms reverted. They collapsed, restored as elf women but unconscious, exhausted from centuries of torment. The party now finds themselves in the cellar of the shrine, with two newly restored figures lying before them—and no clear sense yet of what comes next.
