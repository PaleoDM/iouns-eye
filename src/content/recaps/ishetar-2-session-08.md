### Begin level 5

We opened with our heroes resting near the Braided Spear, when during the watch, Thump and Vicas witnessed something strange in the night sky—a meteor shower, followed by a **single large skyrock** that burned low across the horizon and crashed to the south. Interpreting it as an ill omen, the group set off toward the **Helm-Faced Pool**, one of the three sites said to hold the lost relics of Kareth Varn.

There, they found a series of serene, mist-covered pools surrounded by rock, but their reflections in the water quickly began to misbehave—**mocking them**, chiding them, and telling them to leave. These turned out to be **trickster spirits**, guardians of the helm. When the party refused to back down, the spirits escalated the challenge, summoning two wraiths and several skeletal soldiers.

The fight was short but intense. Rooker locked down the enemy with a well-placed *Tasha’s Hideous Laughter*. Thump deployed *Moonbeam* and Lyra conjured her *Spiritual Weapon*. Vicas and Azrael cleaned up enemies in melee while Lyra delivered key healing spells at critical moments. In the end, the enemies were defeated, and the party claimed **Arbiter’s Shroud**, the helm once worn by the Warden Unyielding.

Before fading, the spirits warned that their third counterpart, guardian of the final relic **Dawncatcher**, had come under attack that very morning. The party concluded the **meteor** must be connected, and made haste to the final site: the **Breached Hollow**.

There, among the ruins of what once looked like simple farmsteads, they found something horrible. **Starspawn**, twisted extraterrestrial beings, were tearing apart the remains of the final lieutenant. The smaller creatures were humanoid but bloated, with spiked joints and unnatural movement. The larger ones were monstrous—centipede-limbed, four-armed, fanged, and cruel.

The fight was brutal. The starspawn struck from the shadows and attacked multiple times per turn. Rooker tried to turn the tide with a *Darkness* spell but was downed in the confusion. Thump conjured wolves and scrambled to stabilize the field. Lyra cast *Spirit Guardians*, controlling space and slowing the enemies, while also maintaining healing support. Azrael, true to form, charged ahead and ended up fighting alone, only to be saved by Vicas, who rushed to their aid. Through clutch heals and a few critical hits, the tide turned, and the enemies were destroyed.

With the lieutenant slain, the **Dawncatcher** was recovered. Our heroes returned to the site of Kareth Varn’s resting place and presented all three relics: **Verity’s Edge**, **Arbiter’s Shroud**, and **Dawncatcher**. With the items reunited, and with Vicas and the others having demonstrated that someone new had taken up the mantle of duty, **Kareth was finally convinced to rest.**

As he faded, the two remaining skeletal lieutenants pledged themselves to continue watching over the Red Vale—not as warriors, but as guardians of memory, serving as the material components for Thump and Rooker's summoning spells. The oaths were finally released.

Our heroes returned to the vegepygmy village to make their final farewells to Sprigg, recover Pinto, and from there, began the journey back to Ishetar. They returned with a small group of vegepygmy citizens ready to learn the ways of the Astorian people. In the weeks that followed, the party debriefed Lady Rowena and helped begin preparations for winter. We join our heroes after a few weeks of downtime as they settle in to a little rest and begin building up newly formalized bastions in anticipation of a harsh winter to come.
