We rejoined our heroes as they guided the *Brass Wing* into the foothills of the Riftkeep Mountains in search of a roc egg. Early exploration was cautious—they avoided two particularly dangerous obstacles: the Eye of the Nestmother (a hovering stone island watched over by a massive, spiritual roc matriarch), and a powerful isolated storm cell that nearly sent the ship tumbling. Once inside the nesting cliffs, they found an unguarded tree-top nest perched over a sheer drop. As the roc mother was away, the party made their descent via foot powered copter.

At the base of the tree, they discovered multiple eggs—massive and precariously placed. Thorn resolved the question of how to retrieve them by activating a powerful new ability, transforming into a 20-foot-tall juggernaut and scaling the tree with ease. Veil provided support from the ground while the others stayed alert—but before they could secure their prize, a clutch of wyverns descended.

The battle was fast and brutal. Thorn took the brunt of a poisoned critical hit but tanked it through sheer bulk. Arden scattered one wyvern with a *Dissonant Whispers* spell, and Veil and Ty coordinated with Shooka to quickly identify two safe (unfertilized) eggs. With danger mounting, the team elected to flee. Arden’s *Mantle of Inspiration* and Veil’s mobility helped everyone withdraw as the wyverns turned their attention to the remaining eggs in the nest. The party hustled back to their extraction point, only to realize the *Brass Wing* wasn’t yet in position. They waited at their small descent copter for extraction—and that’s when things took a turn.

What appeared to be another racing crew closed in fast. But the truth was far worse: they were yugoloth mercenaries in disguise, hired by the rakshasa Haji—the same fiend once summoned by the Hushed Choir. Here now for revenge, they struck without warning. The yugoloth leader, a Nykaloth, tore into the crew with a vicious greataxe while two mezzoloths backed him up.

The battle was near disastrous. Arden cast *Heat Metal* on the axe, forcing disadvantage on the Nykaloth’s strikes, while Veil dashed through the battlefield to administer potions and stabilize allies. Ty’s *Faerie Fire* set the stage for a devastating critical hit that did over 80 damage in one round. Quinn launched a barrage of *Chromatic Orbs*, including a risky triple-cast after accepting a devil’s bargain from the DM. It paid off—barely. Multiple party members dropped unconscious during the fight, including Arden and Quin, but timely revivals and coordinated tactics saw the fiends eventually brought down. Veil finished things off with a powerful air gust that exploded the carapace off of the last Mezzoloth.

As the *Brass Wing* swooped in for extraction, the party scrambled aboard and took flight. They narrowly avoided another pass through the gravity-twisting Mistrise as they made their way back toward Rifthaven. The current standings: the *Burnished Thorn* and *Opal Gale* remain slightly ahead (but heavily damaged), the *Dagger’s Wake* is still combing the hills for an egg, and *The Rustwood* and *The Breath of Mourn* linger near the finish line, waiting to steal a win by force.

We join our heroes on the final leg of the Roc Run, eggs in tow, battered and bruised but still in the race.
