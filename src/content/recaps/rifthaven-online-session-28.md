We joined our heroes caught, quite literally, between a horde of Kobolds and a pack of vicious monsters, called Kruthiks. Each party was bearing down on our heroes, seemingly intent on discerning whether they were friend or foe, while suspiciously assuming that our heroes were reinforcements for the other side.

Quin and Ty found themselves negotiating with the kobolds, by virtue of Quin's ability to speak Draconic. The kobolds insisted that they must be great hunters to have made it this far, and issued them a challenge. Ty attempted to bluff, insinuating that he had been chosen by "her majesty the great scaled queen". It did not go well.

Meanwhile, Thorn and Veil were desperately trying to convince a pair of Kruthik elders that they were not kobolds. Things were looking good when they pointed out that none in the party had a tail, nor really any scales. But the success was short lived when the Kruthiks mentioned a duty to "Ozythar the Tyrant" and suddenly violence was in the air.

In another timeline, our heroes might have successfully been able to extract themselves and let the two parties settle their differences. But here and now, they found themselves besieged on all sides. Veil and Thorn held off the Kruthiks while Ty cast a Wall of Fire to divide them. This gave Veil and Thorn a great opportunity to push and pull the Kruthiks into the wall constantly, triggering massive damage.

Unfortunately, on the other side of the battle, Quin was fighting for his life against multiple hordes of kobolds. He was holding his own alright, but soon found himself overwhelmed. Arden's healing came into play just in time. But even more importantly, our heroes finished off the last oft he Kruthiks, giving them an opening with which to parlay with th kobolds.

What proceeded was the most unusual tea party, in which the kobolds shared with them something they seem to hold quite precious: water. During this time, our heroes learned that the kobolds are absolutely certain that their dragon queen still lives. They insist that even though none of them have spoken with her personally, that everyone knows someone who's spoken with her.

The kobolds were able to give our heroes the lay of the land a bit, showing several adjoining rooms in the cavern system here. They also told our heroes quite simply: they plant o launch an all out offensive on Kruthik territory and use it to push deeper than they've ever gone. Our heroes, they suggested, could join, or they could go speak to the kobold council of elders which, our heroes had bluffed was their original intention.

The party decided that no other quest where was as vital as eliminating the suspected beholder, Ozythar. Thus, they decided to help their new kobold allies push as deep into Kruthik territory as possible and use it as a staging ground to plan their assault on the eye tyrant himself. Lingering in the periphery however, are concerns about the kobolds, the dragon queen, and rumors of Silverwerave Ventures legionnaires somewhere in these caverns...

We join our heroes mid combat, having helped the kobolds launch their offensive. Thanks to some fortunate rolls, the party has identified a key choke point and successfully drawn several kruthik agents out: dragon-like dinosaurs that seem to have been twisted and warped by the eye tyrant. Should our heroes succeed in taking this chamber, it would be the furthest that kobolds have ever pushed into Kruthik territory...but then the question remains: can they hold it?
