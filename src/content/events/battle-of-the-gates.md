## What Happened

Sallow's forces reached Ishetar and launched a multi-front assault on the town's gates. The party split across the three gates to reinforce the defenders. Azrael and Thump held the South Gate, where Azrael provided aerial bombardment and Thump convinced many corrupted creatures to turn back. Rooker and Vicas took the East Gate, undermining enemy morale through potions and suggestive magic. Lyra held the North Gate with Vero and Sombra, using Calm Emotions to break the attackers' fervor.

The defenders won a resounding victory at the gates. Sallow then arrived personally, piloting a juggernaut: a hybrid awakened tree and mechanical war machine. Hrothgar baited the juggernaut into a dwarven pit trap by letting it step on him. The machine is now rooted in place.

## Consequences

The party is charging the trapped juggernaut to confront Sallow directly. Hrothgar's fate is unknown. The juggernaut won't stay stuck forever, giving the party a narrow window to end this.
