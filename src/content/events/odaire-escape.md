## Summary

After a Shadar-kai assassination forced the party through a portal into the Shadowfell, they found themselves in Odaire, a Domain of Dread ruled by Maligno, a sentient carrionette who traps children in an eternal puppet kingdom. Lulu recognized the domain and led the party to the Faerie Godmother, her auntie, who provided crucial guidance.

The party navigated Odaire disguised as poppets and teenagers, rescued the Birthday Host (later named Turmoil) from Maligno's theater, and defeated the Poppet Master. From Giuseppe the Toymaker they obtained a compass to return to Shadow Rifthaven, and from Maligno's defeat they claimed the Curtain's Call lance and a calling card attuned to the Pallbearer's domain. Sessions 37 through 41.
