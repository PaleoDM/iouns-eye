## Summary

Rifthaven's legendary airship race, the Roc Run, took place across Sessions 15 through 17. The party joined Captain Lysa Vex's crew aboard the Brass Wing, with each member filling a shipboard role. They survived yugoloth mercenaries sent by the rakshasa Haji, navigated the treacherous Rustwood, and won the race, returning to the Harrowside Docks with two unfertilized roc eggs.

At the victory banquet, the head of the House of Coinwrights revealed himself as Haji the rakshasa, delivering a veiled warning to the party. The firbolg Boreal of the Earthen Loam also approached Thorn at the celebration.
