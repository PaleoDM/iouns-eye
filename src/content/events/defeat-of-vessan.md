## What Happened

The party confronted Vessan at the oracle shrine. The ancient celestial being, twisted by centuries of isolation, intended to use infant Ellie as a vessel to rupture divine energy across the land. Azrael struck a surprise blow, and Vessan transformed into a magnificent giant elk accompanied by four divine sparks. The party destroyed all forms, with Lyra landing the killing blow on the final wisp.

## Consequences

Saved Quetzi's life and prevented a divine catastrophe. However, the wards on the divine well continue to fail, and wild divinity will leak into Ekkorai regardless.
