## What Happened

The party teleported to Manor Harsk to extract Castellan Drev's family before the Guildmasters could use them as leverage. They found the estate seized by Lady Callista Vohn, the headservant Bessa executed and piked, and Isla and Lisel under house arrest. The family was scattered across three locations, forcing the party to split: Azrael and Vicas infiltrated the manor, Thump and the Castellan went to the fields, and Lyra and Rooker went into town.

All three extractions succeeded. Callista was beaten, her disguised soldiers in the fields were exposed and defeated, and a thug threatening Rekka was neutralized. Lady Isla burned the manor rather than leave it for the enemy. The Castellan modified Udo's memory to protect him, and the party teleported to the Bank of Zoink.

## Consequences

The Harsk family is safe but dispossessed. Udo believes the entire family died in the fire, providing cover. The party gained six feldunost and a disillusioned Dawnfather acolyte. The Guildmasters have lost their leverage over the Castellan.
