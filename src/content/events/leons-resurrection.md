## Summary

After the failed resurrection ritual in Session 45, Leon's soul coalesced into a Pleb gemstone inside the diamond Ty had used. He could see, hear, and speak, but was trapped as a budget soul gem. The luck check came back a 7.

Back in Rifthaven, Freddie and Ty rebuilt Leon into a construct-like form. What came back was not the paladin who walked into the Shadowfell. It was closer to a construct piloting Leon's armor: one wing broken, one functional, a slightly undead disposition. More machine than man. But he was back, and his paladin aura still held when the yugoloths attacked the bastion later that session.
