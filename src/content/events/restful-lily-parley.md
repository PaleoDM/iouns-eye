## What Happened

The party hosted a diplomatic parley with the TTT delegation at the Restful Lily. Warden Brenna Tolvane led the TTT contingent, accompanied by Verlaine Luthaire posing as a trade liaison. The confrontation exposed the TTT's intentions and set the stage for the compound assault.

## Consequences

Led directly to the party's reconnaissance and eventual assault on the TTT compound. Verlaine's true identity as a Celestial Seed agent was not yet revealed at this point.
