## What Happened

The party uncovered an assassination conspiracy against Duchess Lyla of Emberspost, orchestrated by the Seven Eyes network through the corrupted priest Father Assam and agent Vicar Xona. The party defeated the conspirators and saved the Duchess. Lolth herself intervened to hang Vicar Xona for overstepping.

## Consequences

Emberspost was stabilized politically. Father Assam was placed under church custody. The party gained sea passage and political allies. The Seven Eyes network was partially exposed.
