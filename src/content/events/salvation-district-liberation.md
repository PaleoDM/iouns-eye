## Summary

Spanning Sessions 18 through 36, the Salvation District arc saw the party ally with the de la Alma family to overthrow Lord Sergente's Coinwright-backed plantation operation. The mountainous agricultural zone was run on oppressed labor, with powerful landowners controlling the economy on behalf of the House of Coinwrights.

The party assaulted the Moving Convoy, a massive wheeled fortress used to haul prisoners, and later infiltrated the plantation manor. They rescued RELIC prisoners, exposed Dr. Sprocket's body augmentation experiments, destroyed Haji's Arcane Forge to cripple the rakshasa's deal with Mammon, and ultimately liberated the district from Coinwright control.
