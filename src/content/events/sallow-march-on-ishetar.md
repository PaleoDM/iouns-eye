## What Happened

With the TTT compound's leadership in chaos, Sallow departed north with approximately 30 soldiers. Castellan Drev received and read aloud orders from Guildmaster Lucan Tesseri to conquer Ishetar and seize an infant — revealing Sallow's true mission is kidnapping Ellie Caldrow for the Celestial Seed's Convergence ritual. Drev declared "I can't do any of this" and proposed naming Kess Vauthi as new Timbermaster to avert bloodshed.

## Consequences

Ishetar faces an imminent military threat. The party must decide whether to stabilize the compound or race north to intercept Sallow. The campaign's central crisis converges: military, political, spiritual, and personal stakes all collide.
