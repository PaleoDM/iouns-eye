## What Happened

The party responded to Meris Caldrow's desperate plea at Dirty Gerty's to rescue her husband Dalrick, brother-in-law Bran, and children Jora and Mickell from the oracle shrine north of Ishetar. They battled ogres on the hunting paths, received a magical communication from the couatl Quetzi warning that time was short, and arrived at the shrine to find the civilians tied around a stone dais.

## Consequences

Introduced the party to the divine well's failing wards, the threat of wild divinity leaking into Ekkorai, and the infant Ellie Caldrow's significance as an Aasimar.
