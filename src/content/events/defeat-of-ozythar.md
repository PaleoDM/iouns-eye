## Summary

The battle against the beholder Ozythar in the Boughshadow Caverns was one of the campaign's defining moments. The party descended through kobold-infested cavern systems, fought beholder-kin guardians, and ultimately confronted the Tyrant in its lair of jagged islands suspended over darkness.

The fight was devastating. Both Arden and Quin fell during the battle, with Quin disintegrated by Ozythar's eye ray, reducing him to ash. Arden died a poignant death he found worthy of song. The surviving party members, Ty, Veil, and Thorn, broke through the beholder's defenses, and Ty landed the killing blow. Arden was later resurrected, but Quin's death without a body made recovery far more complicated.
