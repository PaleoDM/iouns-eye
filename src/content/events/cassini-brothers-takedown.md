## Summary

The takedown of the Cassini Brothers crime family in the Sparkworks Tunnels, executed across Sessions 49 and 50. The operation began with the unmasking of Major Dunthar's ambush in Session 49 and culminated in the battle at the Rum Runner Aquifer in Session 50, where the party intercepted Oleg and Perry Ellis Cassini attempting to move 111 memory crystals through their own smuggling tunnels and into the Nine Hells.

The Cassini Brothers fielded a crew of thugs and projected a short-range necrotic aura that prevented healing for anyone caught in melee. Both Thorn and Ty were brought to zero hit points inside the aura. Veil grappled Ty out of the field so Leon could revive him. Thorn refused death three separate times via Relentless Rage and Rage Beyond Death. Freddie raised zombies to collect the floating crates of memory crystals before they could be lost to the river. Perry Ellis fell first; Leon's smites ended Oleg.

All 111 souls were recovered. Veil took on the work of returning them, eventually plane-shifting the crystals with Freddie to Captain Alistair, who could carry them across to the afterlife.

The takedown removed the last of Haji's "three stools" of leverage in Rifthaven, leaving the rakshasa isolated and without infrastructure to move souls to the Hells.
