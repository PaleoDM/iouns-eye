## Summary

In the Boughshadow Caverns, the party confronted Silverweave Ventures' forces who were attempting to commodify the Tree of Ambition, an ancient crystal tree with a quartz trunk, gypsum leaves, opal flowers, and basaltic roots. Commander Oswald led the Silverweave legionnaires and deployed an extraction apparatus to harness the tree's power.

The battle was fierce, with Oswald landing vicious critical hits and charming party members with his blade. After defeating Oswald and his forces, Thorn charged the apparatus and destroyed it by sinking his magic-disabling blade into the crystal sphere. The tree's guardian spirit, Rosie, gifted Thorn the Lore-Axe for his protection of the tree.
