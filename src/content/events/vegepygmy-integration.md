## What Happened

After traveling through the Red Vale and meeting the vegepygmy settlement, the party negotiated an oath-bound citizenship agreement with Elder Sprigg. The deal was sealed with magical brands on Sprigg's and Rooker's palms. The vegepygmies would receive citizenship and protection in exchange for winter food production and mutual defense.

## Consequences

Ishetar gained a new population of citizens, agricultural capabilities for winter food production, and intelligence about the Red Vale. The vegepygmies began growing fungal structures alongside human infrastructure.
