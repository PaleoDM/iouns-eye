## Summary

The liberation of the Sparkworks District was the culmination of the party's first major arc, spanning Sessions 1 through 12. Hired by the tiefling Dante to investigate smuggling tunnels beneath the impoverished district, the party uncovered and restored the Great Self fungal colony, re-established the old truce with the Sparkplugs, and ultimately drove the Hushed Choir cult from the Hearthfire Commons tenements.

The party earned the community's trust and were gifted Lord Harwin's brother's old manor as their base of operations, known thereafter as the Bastion. The victory also established Sparkworks as the party's home district, where they built out extensive Bastion facilities and recruited a local militia.
