## Summary

After being pressured by the Cassini Brothers to pull a job, Quin convinced the party to instead heist the stolen memory the Brothers held over him as collateral. Quin crafted a persona, "The Great Memo (Memorio)," disguising himself as a high roller to talk his way into the Cassini vault.

The plan worked, and Quin won big enough to attract the attention of Oleg Cassini. Inside the vault, the party also encountered the unsettling Dr. Solvane. Veil learned that, in addition to memories, the Cassini Brothers trade in trapped souls, preventing resurrection and selling control of those souls to Rifthaven's elite.
