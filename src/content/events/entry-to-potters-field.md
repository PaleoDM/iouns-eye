## Summary

After escaping Odaire, the party used the Pallbearer's calling card to press through the mists and emerged at a sad dockside town in the Shadowfell. The ghost ship Dancing Dirge, captained by Alistair (revealed as Ty's biological father), carried them to Potter's Field, the Pallbearer's Domain of Dread, a capitalist afterlife where souls are traded as currency.

To mask their living presence from the Pallbearer, the party hunted a shadow leviathan and applied its blood. At the domain's customs house, they were required to declare magic items and offer personal collateral. In Sessions 42 through 43, they defeated Divine Judge Josephine Maren and discovered that Quin's soul portrait is held in the Pallbearer's personal Galleria, setting up a heist to recover it.
