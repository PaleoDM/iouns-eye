## Summary

During the assault on the Moving Convoy in the Salvation District, the party discovered Dr. Sprocket conducting body augmentation experiments on RELIC prisoners. Sprocket's journal revealed that Lord Sergente and a figure known as "The Dividend" were behind the operation, using the de la Alma family's youngest as a test subject.

Thorn found a cell housing four RELIC prisoners: Rust, Clubs, Rider, and Spirit. By invoking Sartori's true name, Relic Beta07, he earned their immediate trust. The rescue was commissioned by Sartori, the stylish RELIC artisan behind Sartori's Arcane Outfitters.
