## Summary

During the escape from Potter's Field, the party confronted the Pallbearer directly at the docks. After refusing his offer to trade Captain Alistair's soul for safe passage, combat broke out against the Pallbearer, his spirit naga mount, and four death knights.

The party burned through the Pallbearer's legendary resistances. Veil stunned and grappled him, and Ty struck with a Psychic Lance. Leon moved in for the killing blow, drawn in part by the platinum that enemies had been exploding into on death. The Pallbearer's death triggered a Power Word Kill contingency. Leon failed his save and died instantly.

Ty performed a resurrection ritual on the deck of the Dancing Dirge. Freddie lowered the DC from 10 to 7. Thorn contributed, lowering it to 6. Veil presented Art Doyle's portrait, and Doyle spoke with Leon in death — the two debated whether anything was worth coming back for. The DC dropped to 3. Ty's player rolled the final check: a 2.

Leon died one point short of returning. His player is exploring a revenant return to finish the mission against the Aletheia Procedure.
