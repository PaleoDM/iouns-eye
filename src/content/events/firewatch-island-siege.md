## What Happened

The party arrived at Firewatch Island monastery to find it under siege by an undead army emerging from the sea. The abbot was dead. The party fought undead creatures and escaped with survivors, learning that the assault was connected to High Prince Brof's deal with the demon lord Syrgaul.

## Consequences

Revealed the scale of the undead threat to the Ishetar region. Established the Rift as a dangerous location the monastery was meant to guard. The undead invasion timeline remains unknown.
