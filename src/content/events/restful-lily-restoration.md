## What Happened

The party discovered the Restful Lily sanctuary in the Myreshade Forest and broke the curses afflicting its founders — Sylvari (transformed into a medusa) and Solara (transformed into a lamia). With the curses lifted, the sanctuary was fully restored and consecrated.

## Consequences

Created a critical safe haven and diplomatic neutral ground in western Ekkorai. Established Ambrosia as a root-network sentinel and Cyrena's hot springs as a source of Greater Restoration. The site later hosted the parley with the TTT.
