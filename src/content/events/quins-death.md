## Summary

During the battle against the beholder Ozythar in the Boughshadow Caverns, Quin was struck by a Disintegration Ray and reduced to ash. Without a body, only powerful ritual magic or a dangerous bargain could hope to bring him back. His dust was gathered by the surviving party members, though perhaps not enough to easily anchor a soul.

Quin's death triggered a crisis within the Aletheia Procedure. Leon's Justicar blade extinguished Quin's name without a proper collection, an impossibility according to the order's rules. This contradiction led Sir Sunder to investigate, uncovering potential corruption connected to the Pallbearer in the Shadowfell. The quest to recover Quin's soul ultimately drove the party into the Domains of Dread.
