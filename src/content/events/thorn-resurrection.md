## Summary

During the early days of the Sparkworks revolution, Thorn fell in battle against Hushed Choir cultists near the Ashen Chapel. Sister Ellara revived him and administered a Cure Wounds just in time, as the cultists were simultaneously launching a public assault against the chapel and the growing civil unrest in the district.
