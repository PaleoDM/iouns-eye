## Summary

In 1082 TA, coordinated sahuagin raids and goblin attacks threatened the colony of Ishetar. During the siege, the party uncovered the Deep Scion spy Fisherman Dyrk, who had been infiltrating the colony disguised as a common fisherman. His discovery and elimination helped turn the tide against the attackers.
