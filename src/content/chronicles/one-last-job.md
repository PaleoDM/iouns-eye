## Summary
An in-character narrative written from the perspective of Maeve Willowspell, chronicling the party's mission for the Peregrine Society to investigate giant attacks across the Cradle of Conflict. The story covers the shattering of the Ordning, confrontations with giant lords, the revelation of Iymrith's deception, the rescue of King Hekkathon, and the final defeat of both Iymrith and the primordial kraken Slarkrethel.

## Notable Details
- The Peregrine Society serves as the primary quest-giving organization for small-folk heroes
- The Ordning is a cosmic law governing giant hierarchy and behavior
- Iymrith's disguise as a storm giant advisor demonstrates the depth of draconic shapeshifting magic
- Slarkrethel's manipulation happens entirely from the deep ocean, suggesting vast psychic powers
