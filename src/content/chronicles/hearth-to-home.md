## Summary

An in-character diary spanning 4th Moradin 1088 to 22nd Avandra 1089, written from the perspective of Kip the gnome druid. The narrative covers the party's arrival at New Perile Falls, investigations into the cult of Grave Dirt, exploration of the Kuo-Toa power plant, the sunken ruins of Old Perile Falls, the defeat of Vespa and Drelduin, and the infiltration of Orbos. Kip's entries are deeply personal, reflecting on his connection to nature and the Hearth, his growing divine relationship with Glittergold, and the moral complexities of soul-bearing constructs and divine contracts.

## Notable Details

- Kip's Hearth is a sacred sanctuary for animals with a planar barrier and portal to the Feywild
- Glittergold reveals to Kip that someone tricked the gods into releasing dragons, including the Void Dragon
- The divine contract in Mechanus governs how gods can intervene; someone exploited loopholes
- Priests lost their magic after the Fall of Old Perile Falls as divine punishment for Project Anima
- Kip develops the ability to hold animal forms for extended periods
