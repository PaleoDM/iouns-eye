A powerful criminal organization in Rifthaven that operated gambling halls and traded in stolen memories and trapped souls. The Cassini Brothers held leverage over those they did business with, using stolen memories as collateral to force compliance. Among Rifthaven's upper crust, control of trapped souls was a lucrative commodity.

Oleg and Perry Ellis Cassini ran operations directly, employing enforcers like Major Dunthar. The organization held Quin's stolen memories as leverage until the party executed a heist to recover them.

In Session 50, the brothers were caught at the Rum Runner Aquifer attempting to smuggle 111 memory crystals through the Sparkworks Tunnels to deliver to Haji in the Nine Hells. Both brothers were killed in the engagement, dismantling the organization and removing the last of Haji's three "stools" of leverage in Rifthaven.
