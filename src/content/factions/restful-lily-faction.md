## Purpose & Goals

A consecrated sanctuary serving as a safe haven and diplomatic neutral ground in the Myreshade Forest. Offers healing, respite, and Greater Restoration through Cyrena's hot springs.

## Key Members

Co-founded by the elven sisters Sylvari (Moonweaver) and Solara (Dawnfather), with Ambrosia (awakened tree guardian) and Cyrena (water nymph). Strongly allied with Ishetar, especially through Thump's root-link with Ambrosia.

## Role in Campaign

Hosted the critical diplomatic parley between Ishetar and the TTT (Session 27). Serves as the party's western outpost and early warning system via Ambrosia's root network.
