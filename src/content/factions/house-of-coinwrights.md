One of the premier money-lending and banking institutions in Rifthaven, the House of Coinwrights wields enormous political and economic power across the city. Their influence extends into the lower districts through intermediaries, funding operations like the Hushed Choir cult in Sparkworks and backing Lord Sergente's plantation operations in the Salvation District.

The head of the House of Coinwrights is the rakshasa Haji, who revealed himself at the Roc Run victory banquet. Haji has been seeking a loan from Mammon, lord of the Third Layer of the Hells, using an Arcane Forge as proof of concept for a larger scheme.

After Session 50, Veil began building the case implicating the House in Haji's operation. With the Cassini Brothers dismantled, the Pallbearer deposed, and Vine Ashen Bloom exposed, the House's last remaining lever for moving souls to the Hells has been knocked away.
