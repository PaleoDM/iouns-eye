A shadowy cult that operated in the Sparkworks District of Rifthaven, identifiable by their unsettling mouthless masks. The Hushed Choir terrorized the district's residents and sought to control the smuggling tunnels beneath the district for unknown purposes. Ledgers recovered after their defeat linked their funding to the House of Coinwrights in the upper districts.

The cult was destroyed during the liberation of Sparkworks in 1128 TA, when the party drove them from the Hearthfire Commons tenements and killed their leadership, including Vandro Morrix and Seressa Vale.
