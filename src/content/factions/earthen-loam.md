A mysterious druid organization encountered by the party at the Roc Run victory banquet. The firbolg Boreal, a member of the Earthen Loam, approached Thorn and gently probed his interest in joining. She wore a pin matching a scrying crystal the party had previously found.

Boreal left the door open for Thorn to join but warned him to destroy the scrying crystal if he valued his privacy. The full extent of the Earthen Loam's goals and operations remains unknown.
