The Order of the Ju-Shi is a holy military order pledged to all fourteen Prime Deities. Composed of Clerics, Paladins, and Druids, they are dedicated to fighting corrupted deities and demons. Their headquarters is in Rifthaven.

In Session 48, the Ju-Shi monastery convened its first tribunal in over sixty years after Divine Judge Vine Ashen Bloom had Sir Sunder arrested on fabricated charges. The tribunal voted overwhelmingly to prosecute Vine for her collusion with the House of Coinwrights and the Pallbearer, and the Order cast its vote of no confidence against the Aletheia Procedure, freezing the system pending governmental review.
