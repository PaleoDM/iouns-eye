A commercial faction that sent legionnaires into the Boughshadow caverns beneath Rifthaven to commodify the Tree of Ambition, one of the ancient crystal trees entrusted to nature guardians. Their soldiers were led by a commander named Oswald and deployed an apparatus designed to extract or harness the tree's power.

Silverweave's operations were dismantled when the party defeated Oswald and his forces, and Thorn destroyed the extraction apparatus. Nanny Pu'Pu, the ancient witch of Boughshadow, claimed to have eaten some of their scouts.
