## Purpose & Goals

A fey court of beings called "folk who are not mostly fae" — mortals transformed by fey influence. Allied with Ishetar after the party safely returned two of their members, Minor Key and Major Chord, from a hell hound den (Session 3).

## Key Members

Led by Lord Crescendo, who sent three blink dogs as a gift to Azrael via a Quickling (Session 26). A carved message near Azrael's kennel reads: "The Court of Thorns remembers its friends. A seat has been held."

## Relationship with Party

The Thornsong holds a debt of gratitude toward the party and has offered them "a seat" in their court, suggesting a future alliance opportunity.
