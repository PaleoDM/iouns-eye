## Purpose & Goals

An extraterrestrial species that arrived in Ekkorai via the "sky rock" that created the Red Vale. They speak of a "Maggot Daddy" and have integrated into Ishetar as citizens under an oath-bound agreement with the party (Session 6).

## Integration

Sealed with magical brands on Sprigg's and Rooker's palms. In exchange for citizenship and protection, the vegepygmies provide winter vegetable production, fungal agricultural knowledge, and regional intelligence. They are growing fungal structures in Ishetar alongside human infrastructure.

## Population

Approximately 30-50 integrated into Ishetar, with more possibly remaining in the Red Vale.
