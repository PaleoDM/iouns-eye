The crew of the airship Brass Wing, captained by Lysa Vex. The Brass Wing was originally a mercantile company that owned an entire fleet of corsair ships, including the one Arden had previously served on. After the death of Seressa Vale, the former owner revealed to be a Hushed Choir leader, Captain Vex took command.

The party served as crew aboard the Brass Wing during the Roc Run, with Arden as First Mate, Thorn as Bosun, Ty as Navigator, Veil as Medic, and Quin as Cook. They won the race and successfully retrieved two unfertilized roc eggs.
