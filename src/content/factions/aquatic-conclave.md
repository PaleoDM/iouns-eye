## Purpose & Goals

An alliance of underwater peoples — Locathah, Sea Elves, Tritons, Merfolk, Merrow, and potentially Sahuagin — convening to discuss the undead threat emerging from the sea.

## Leadership

Overseen by Tidemark Janus, a triton authority who coordinates aquatic forces and assigned bodyguards Dagas and Uthe to Captain Alba. The conclave's political outcome regarding engagement with surface conflicts remains undecided.
