## Purpose & Goals

A commercial logging operation contracted by the Aroiin Empire (via the Guildsmen merchant class) to exploit the timber resources of Ekkorai. Chartered by Emperor Beluar Aroiin in 1080 TA.

## Structure

Led by Warden Brenna Tolvane (fled), with lieutenants Sallow and Fen Galloway, Castellan Drev Harsk (defected), war mage Magister Yara Osei, Sergeant Tormund "Stump" Barren, and Quartermaster Kess Vauthi. Houses approximately 200 laborers and families with 25-30 combatants plus 30 more marching north under Sallow.

## Methods

Uses corrupted druid magic for extraction — reversed Plant Growth, forced growth cycles that hollow trees from within, beast spy networks, and blood components. The Myreshade Forest is dying as a result.

## Current State

Leadership fractured after the party's assault (Session 29). Tolvane and Veraine fled south. Drev switched allegiance to the party. Sallow marches north with 30 soldiers. Kess Vauthi may step into the power vacuum.
