The Ledgermen were the corrupt local militia of the Sparkworks District, serving as enforcers who harassed residents and maintained order on behalf of powerful interests. They had a particular vendetta against the tiefling Dante and targeted anyone who associated with him.

The Ledgermen were neutralized during the liberation of Sparkworks in 1128 TA after Lord Harwin Brassfell borrowed heavily from the Cassini Brothers to buy them off, allowing the party to focus on defeating the Hushed Choir.
