## Description

Moradin, the All-Hammer, is the god of craft and creation. He forged the dwarves from stone and fire, and his followers believe that the act of creation is the highest form of prayer.

## Worship & Followers

Dwarves, smiths, masons, and craftspeople of all kinds venerate the All-Hammer. His temples are forges and workshops where the faithful create.

## Holy Day Traditions

Deep Solace falls on the 18th of Moradin. A day of isolation and meditation on family, followed by the exchange of handcrafted gifts.
