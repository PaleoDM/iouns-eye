## Description

The Progenitor, embodiment of raw ferocity and might. Baernoloth represents the savage, physical power of the yugoloths — brute force distilled into fiendish form.

## Domain in Gehenna

Commands the physical legions of Gehenna, marshaling yugoloth forces with overwhelming might.

## Relationships

Works alongside Altraloth and Oinoloth, providing the muscle to their magic and schemes.
