## Description

Archduke of Avernus, the first layer of the Nine Hells. Zariel was once an angel who fell to corruption, and now wages the eternal Blood War against the demons of the Abyss.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Avernus, the first layer — a blasted wasteland of war and ruin, the front line of the eternal Blood War between devils and demons.

## Relationships

Commands the infernal legions in the Blood War. Her fall from angelic grace makes her both pitied and feared.
