## Description

Goddess of deceit and spiders. The Spider Queen is the matron of drow, who betrayed Corellon the Archheart and was cast into darkness.

## Worship & Followers

Drow, assassins, and those who spin webs of manipulation serve the Spider Queen. Her temples are hidden in the Underdark.

## Relationships

Betrayed Corellon and was exiled. The drow who followed her became her devoted children in the darkness below.
