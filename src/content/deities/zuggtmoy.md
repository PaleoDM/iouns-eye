## Description

Lady of Fungi, princess of molds and singular consciousness. Zuggtmoy seeks to spread her fungal network across all worlds, linking every living thing into one vast, controlled consciousness.

## Domain in the Abyss

The Mycorealm, a layer of the Abyss blanketed in towering mushrooms and invasive spore clouds.

## Relationships

Shares a strange kinship with Juiblex among the more alien demon princes. Her fungal influence sometimes creeps into Lolth's Underdark domains.
