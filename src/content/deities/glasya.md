## Description

Princess of the Hells and daughter of Asmodeus. Glasya rules Malbolge with cruel authority, specializing in imprisonment and torture.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Malbolge, the sixth layer — a treacherous landscape of steep slopes and crushing gravity.

## Relationships

Daughter of Asmodeus, granted her layer as a test of loyalty. Rivals with neighboring archdevils.
