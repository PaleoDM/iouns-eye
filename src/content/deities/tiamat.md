## Description

Goddess of power and subjugation. The Scaled Tyrant is the progenitor of chromatic dragons and the eternal enemy of Bahamut, the Platinum Dragon.

## Worship & Followers

Chromatic dragons, dragonborn who reject Bahamut, and power-hungry cultists serve the Scaled Tyrant.

## Relationships

Eternal enemy of Bahamut. Kurtulmak serves as her vassal.
