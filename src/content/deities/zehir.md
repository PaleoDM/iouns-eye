## Description

God of assassins, poisons, and snakes. The Cloaked Serpent teaches that the unseen strike is the most powerful — patience and precision over brute force.

## Worship & Followers

Assassins, poisoners, and yuan-ti serve the Cloaked Serpent. His temples are hidden and his rituals silent.

## Relationships

His followers sometimes work alongside those of Lolth and Vecna, united by their preference for shadow over open warfare.
