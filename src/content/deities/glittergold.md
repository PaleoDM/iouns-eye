## Description

Glittergold, the GoldSoul, is the god of pranks, cooperation, and illusion. He is the progenitor of gnomes, who inherited his love of invention, mischief, and collaborative creation.

## Worship & Followers

Gnomes, tinkerers, illusionists, and inventors honor the GoldSoul. His shrines are often hidden in plain sight, revealed only by those clever enough to find them.

## Holy Day Traditions

Fizzleboom falls on the 14th of Glittergold. A day for celebrating failures and lessons learned — communities gather for explosive displays and pipe smoke illusions.
