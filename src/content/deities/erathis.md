## Description

Erathis, the Lawbearer, is the goddess of civilization, law, and peace. The Lawbearer champions order, governance, and the collective good that arises when people build together. Her month falls in autumn.

## Worship & Followers

Judges, lawmakers, city leaders, and those who value civic order pray to the Lawbearer. Her temples are courthouses and civic halls.

## Holy Day Traditions

Harvest Throne falls on the 22nd of Erathis. Elaborate harvest feasts are held, with one seat always left empty for the Lawbearer herself.
