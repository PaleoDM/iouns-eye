## Description

The Dark Prince of hedonism, lust, and indulgence. Graz'zt is the most seductive of the demon princes, ruling through charm and excess rather than brute destruction.

## Domain in the Abyss

The Triple Realm — three interconnected layers of the Abyss that he rules with decadent authority.

## Relationships

Rivals Demogorgon and Orcus for supremacy. His charisma and cunning make him uniquely dangerous among demons.
