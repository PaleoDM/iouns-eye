## Description

God of slavery and torture. The Crawling King lurks in the deepest places beneath the world, delighting in the suffering of captives.

## Worship & Followers

Slavers, torturers, and jailers invoke the Crawling King. His temples are dungeons and torture chambers.

## Relationships

His domain overlaps with Lolth's Underdark territories, creating an uneasy coexistence.
