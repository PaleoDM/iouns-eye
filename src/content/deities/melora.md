## Description

Melora, the Wildmother, is the goddess of wilderness, nature, and the seas. She is revered by sailors, rangers, and druids who draw power from the natural world.

## Worship & Followers

Druids, rangers, sailors, and farmers honor the Wildmother. Her temples are groves, sea cliffs, and wild places untouched by civilization.

## Holy Day Traditions

Wild's Grandeur falls on the 14th of Melora. Followers venture into wild places — sailing, hiking, or simply sitting in nature — to honor the Wildmother's domain.
