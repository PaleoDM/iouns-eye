## Description

God of necromancy and dark arcane arts. The Whispered One hoards forbidden knowledge and teaches that secrets are the truest form of power.

## Worship & Followers

Necromancers, dark wizards, and those who seek forbidden knowledge serve the Whispered One. His cults operate in shadow.

## Relationships

A dark mirror of Ioun — where she shares knowledge freely, Vecna hoards it. His followers and those of Orcus are rivals in the domain of undeath.
