## Description

The Ancient One, oldest of the yugoloth lords. Altraloth wields powerful old magic from before the current age — magic that predates even the prime deities' dominion.

## Domain in Gehenna

Dwells in the deepest reaches of Gehenna, surrounded by wards of ancient power.

## Relationships

The eldest of the three yugoloth lords. Baernoloth and Oinoloth defer to the Ancient One's wisdom, though trust is scarce among fiends.
