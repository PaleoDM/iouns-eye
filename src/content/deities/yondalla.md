## Description

Yondalla, the Luck Spirit, is the goddess of harvest, cheer, and good fortune. She is the progenitor of halflings, who carry her blessing of fortune wherever they wander.

## Worship & Followers

Halflings universally revere Yondalla, along with farmers, innkeepers, and anyone who values community and good cheer.

## Holy Day Traditions

Luck's Favor falls on the 21st of Yondalla, coinciding with full moons. A day of carousing, merry-making, and performing good deeds to earn the Luck Spirit's favor.
