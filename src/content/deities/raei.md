## Description

Raei, the Everlight, is the goddess of atonement and compassion. The Everlight teaches that redemption is possible for all who truly seek it. Her month closes the year, a time for reflection and forgiveness before the new year begins.

## Worship & Followers

Those seeking forgiveness, healers, counselors, and the repentant pray to the Everlight. Her temples are sanctuaries where anyone may seek shelter without judgment.

## Holy Day Traditions

Day of Forgiveness falls on the 14th of Raei. A day for forgiving slights and grudges, exchanging gifts, and making peace before New Year's Day arrives.
