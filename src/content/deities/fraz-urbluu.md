## Description

Prince of deception, master of deceit and illusion. Fraz-Urb'luu is the greatest liar in the Abyss — his illusions are so convincing that even other demon lords fall prey to them.

## Domain in the Abyss

Hollow's Heart, a layer that appears as whatever its inhabitants most desire — until the illusion shatters.

## Relationships

Uses deception rather than force, making him distrusted by every other demon prince.
