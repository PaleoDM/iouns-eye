## Description

God of darkness and destruction. The Chained Oblivion is imprisoned — bound by the other gods for attempting to unmake all of creation. Even other corrupted deities fear his release.

## Worship & Followers

Only the most deranged cultists seek to free the Chained Oblivion. His worship is forbidden and hunted by nearly every faith.

## Relationships

Imprisoned by the collective action of both prime and corrupted deities. His cults operate in utmost secrecy.
