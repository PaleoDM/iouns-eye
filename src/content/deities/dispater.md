## Description

Lord of Dis, master of arms, engineering, and creation. Dispater is a paranoid genius who builds and fortifies endlessly, turning his layer into an impenetrable iron city.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Dis, the second layer — an immense city of iron towers and burning streets, the greatest fortress in the Nine Hells.

## Relationships

Paranoid and reclusive, trusting no one. His engineering prowess makes Dis the most fortified layer of the Hells.
