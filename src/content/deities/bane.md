## Description

God of conquest and tyranny. The Strife Emperor seeks dominion over all — his followers believe strength and subjugation are the natural order.

## Worship & Followers

Tyrants, warlords, and those who crave power over others serve the Strife Emperor.

## Relationships

Allied with other forces of domination. His followers often clash with those of Erathis and Bahamut.
