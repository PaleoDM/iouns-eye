## Description

Corellon, the Archheart, is the god of art and beauty. He is the progenitor of elves, who first emerged from the Feywild under his guidance. Sehanine the Moonweaver is his counterpart.

## Worship & Followers

Elves, artists, musicians, and lovers of beauty venerate the Archheart. His temples are works of art themselves — soaring spires of crystal and living wood.

## Holy Day Traditions

Elvendawn falls on the 21st of Corellon. Elaborate celebrations with music, fine wine, and dancing commemorate the day elves first emerged from the Feywild into Astoria.
