## Description

Kord, the Stormlord, is the god of storm, battle, and competition. The Stormlord values strength tested and proven — not cruelty, but the honest contest of might and will. His month falls in winter.

## Worship & Followers

Warriors, athletes, soldiers, and storm-chasers venerate the Stormlord. His temples are open-air arenas and fighting pits.

## Holy Day Traditions

Day of Challenging falls on the 7th of Kord, during Fool Tides and new moons. Raucous festivals featuring jousting, wrestling, and martial combats. One of the most raucous holidays in Astoria.
