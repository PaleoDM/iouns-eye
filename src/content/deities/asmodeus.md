## Description

Supreme ruler of the Nine Hells. The Lord of Nessus is the most cunning and patient of all evil beings — his plans span millennia and his contracts are inescapable.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Nessus, the ninth and deepest layer of the Nine Hells. From here Asmodeus rules all other archdevils with absolute authority.

## Relationships

All other devil lords serve him, willingly or not. His influence extends into the mortal world through countless infernal contracts.
