## Description

The Schemer, master of cunning and manipulation among the yugoloth lords. Oinoloth plays all sides — devils, demons, and mortals alike — seeking advantage for the yugoloths above all.

## Domain in Gehenna

Operates throughout Gehenna and beyond, weaving plots that span the lower planes.

## Relationships

The most politically active of the yugoloth lords, negotiating mercenary contracts that send yugoloth forces to fight in both the Blood War and mortal conflicts.
