## Description

God of chaos and confusion. The Dismantler seeks to unravel order and structure, sowing discord wherever stability takes root.

## Worship & Followers

Anarchists, saboteurs, and those who thrive in disorder pray to the Dismantler.

## Relationships

Shares a destructive kinship with Tharizdun, though the Dismantler seeks chaos rather than total oblivion.
