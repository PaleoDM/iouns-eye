## Description

Fierna and her father Belial jointly rule Phlegethos. Fierna is the public face — charming, manipulative, and devastatingly cunning — while Belial works from the shadows.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Phlegethos, the fourth layer — a realm of fire, volcanoes, and searing heat where Fierna holds her infernal court.

## Relationships

Rules alongside her father Belial. Her domain of manipulation and cunning trickery makes her a formidable political force among the archdevils.
