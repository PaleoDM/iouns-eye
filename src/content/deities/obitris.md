## Description

Obitris, the Gate Keeper, is the god of death, fate, and winter. The Gate Keeper guards the passage between life and death, ensuring souls reach their proper destination. He is not feared but respected — death is a natural passage, not a punishment. His month falls in autumn.

## Worship & Followers

Morticians, gravediggers, those in mourning, and those who fight undead honor the Gate Keeper. His temples are solemn places of reflection near cemeteries.

## Holy Day Traditions

Gatekeeper's Remembrance falls on the 5th of Obitris, during Fool Tides. A day of remembrance, solemnity, and respect for the fallen.
