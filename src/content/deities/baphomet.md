## Description

Demon prince of hunting, mazes, and labyrinths. Baphomet delights in the hunt — turning his prey loose in impossible labyrinths before running them down.

## Domain in the Abyss

The Endless Maze, a layer of the Abyss consisting of infinite, shifting corridors.

## Relationships

Bitter rival of Yeenoghu. Both compete for influence over savage creatures of the mortal world.
