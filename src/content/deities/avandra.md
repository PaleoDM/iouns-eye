## Description

Avandra, the Changebringer, is the goddess of freedom and escape. She watches over travelers, adventurers, and all who seek new horizons. Her month opens the year and the spring season.

## Worship & Followers

Travelers, adventurers, and those seeking change or new beginnings pray to Avandra. Her shrines are found at crossroads and harbor docks.

## Holy Day Traditions

New Year's Day falls on the 1st of Avandra — the first day of the year. Feasts begin at dusk, and celebrants discuss their hopes for the coming year, talking until sunrise.
