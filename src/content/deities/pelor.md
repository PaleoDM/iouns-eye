## Description

Pelor, the Dawnfather, is the god of healing, warmth, and the sun. He watches over the sick and weary, and his light banishes darkness and undeath.

## Worship & Followers

Healers, farmers, and those who fight undead pray to the Dawnfather. His temples face east to catch the first light of dawn.

## Holy Day Traditions

Highsun falls on the 21st of Pelor — the height of summer. Great feasts are held and enormous bonfires are kept burning through the entire night.
