## Description

Lord of avarice and financer of ruin. Mammon embodies greed in its purest form — every soul has a price, and he delights in finding it.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Minauros, the third layer — a swamp of ooze and filth where treasures sink into the muck, hoarded jealously by Mammon.

## Relationships

His domain of greed overlaps with the corrupted deity Pelferion's province of wealth and oppression.
