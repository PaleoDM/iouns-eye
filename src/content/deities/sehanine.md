## Description

Sehanine, the Moonweaver, is the goddess of illusion, moonlight, and the night. The Moonweaver is the counterpart to Corellon the Archheart. Folklore tells that she braids the three moons of the Celestial Braid every night, as a mother braids a child's hair. Her month opens the winter season.

## Worship & Followers

Lovers, tricksters, travelers by night, and those who seek hidden truths pray to the Moonweaver. Her temples are open-air amphitheaters bathed in moonlight.

## Holy Day Traditions

Moonweaver's Masquerade falls on the 21st of Sehanine, during full moons. A night of song, dance, drink, pranks, tricks, and elaborate masquerades. Young lovers seek the Moonweaver's blessing.
