## Description

The Faceless Lord, prince of oozes and incomprehensible destruction. Juiblex is barely sentient in any way mortals understand — a vast, consuming mass of corrosive slime.

## Domain in the Abyss

The Slime Pits, a layer of the Abyss consisting of nothing but fetid, dissolving ooze.

## Relationships

Even other demon princes avoid Juiblex. Only Zuggtmoy shares any kinship with the Faceless Lord.
