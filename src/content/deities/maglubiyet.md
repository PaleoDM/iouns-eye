## Description

God of union against enemies. The Conqueror is the primary god of goblinoids — goblins, hobgoblins, and bugbears — binding them together through shared hatred of outsiders.

## Worship & Followers

Goblinoids pray to the Conqueror before battle, seeking his blessing to unite their fractious tribes.

## Relationships

His followers often compete with those of Gruumsh for territory and dominance.
