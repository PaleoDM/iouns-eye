## Description

God of wealth and oppression of the masses. The Gilded Overlord teaches that gold is the purest form of control — those who hold the coin hold the power.

## Worship & Followers

Corrupt merchants, exploitative nobles, and those who use wealth as a weapon serve the Gilded Overlord.

## Relationships

A dark reflection of Erathis — where she builds civilization for all, Pelferion builds it to serve the few. His domain overlaps with Mammon's greed in the Nine Hells.
