## Description

God of slaughter and warfare. The Ruiner is the progenitor of orcs, who carry his fury and hunger for destruction. Primary god of orcs.

## Worship & Followers

Orcs, war chiefs, and those who revel in destruction pray to the Ruiner.

## Relationships

Eternal rival of Corellon the Archheart. His followers often clash with those of Kord, who values honorable combat over mindless slaughter.
