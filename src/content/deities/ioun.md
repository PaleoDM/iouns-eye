## Description

Ioun, the Knowing Mistress, is the goddess of knowledge and learning. She watches over all who seek to understand the world and its mysteries. Her eye sees all that is known and all that waits to be discovered.

## Worship & Followers

Scholars, wizards, librarians, and seekers of truth venerate the Knowing Mistress. Her temples are often libraries or repositories of knowledge, open to all who wish to learn.

## Holy Day Traditions

The Knowing falls on the 14th of Ioun's month — the height of summer. Followers spend the day in meditation and reflection, taking stock of what they have learned over the past year and setting intentions for new areas of study.

## Role in Campaigns

This grimoire bears her name — Ioun's Eye — as a repository of all knowledge gathered across the campaigns of Astoria.
