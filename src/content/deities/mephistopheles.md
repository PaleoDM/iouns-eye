## Description

Keeper of souls and hoarder of lore. Mephistopheles rules from a glacial palace in Cania, where he collects souls and forbidden knowledge with equal avarice.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Cania, the eighth layer — a frozen wasteland of ice and bitter cold, dominated by his glacial palace.

## Relationships

The most powerful devil lord beneath Asmodeus, and the one most likely to challenge him. Hoards knowledge that rivals even Vecna's secrets.
