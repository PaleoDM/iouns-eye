## Description

Bahamut, the Platinum Dragon, is the god of honor and justice. The Platinum Dragon is the progenitor of metallic dragons and the eternal enemy of Tiamat, the Scaled Tyrant. His followers uphold justice, protect the innocent, and honor their oaths. His month falls in winter.

## Worship & Followers

Paladins, knights, metallic dragons, and all who champion justice venerate the Platinum Dragon. His temples are grand halls with vaulted ceilings.

## Holy Day Traditions

Embertide falls on the 5th of Bahamut. A solemn day of remembrance for fallen defenders of justice — soldiers, knights, and protectors who gave their lives in service.
