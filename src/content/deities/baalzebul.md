## Description

Lord of lies, bureaucracy, and record keeping. Baalzebul controls information through labyrinthine systems of documentation — truth is buried under layers of procedure.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Maladomini, the seventh layer — a maze of crumbling cities and endless archives where information is both weapon and prison.

## Relationships

Serves Asmodeus but schemes constantly. His bureaucratic domain is a dark mirror of Erathis's civilization.
