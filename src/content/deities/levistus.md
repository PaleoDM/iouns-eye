## Description

The Frozen Prince, imprisoned in an immense block of ice by Asmodeus yet still ruling Stygia through sheer force of will and loyal servants.

## Domain in the Nine Hells

Stygia, the fifth layer — a frozen sea of ice. Levistus himself is entombed within a glacier but commands his domain telepathically.

## Relationships

Imprisoned by Asmodeus for a past betrayal. Despite his confinement, he remains a cunning and dangerous schemer.
