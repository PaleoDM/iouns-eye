## Description

Beast of Butchery, prince of eternal hunger and blood lust. Yeenoghu is the patron of gnolls, driving them into frenzied rampages of slaughter and consumption.

## Domain in the Abyss

The Death Dells, a layer of the Abyss littered with the bones of countless victims.

## Relationships

Bitter rival of Baphomet. His gnoll followers often clash with orc tribes loyal to Gruumsh.
