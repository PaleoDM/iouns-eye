## Description

Prince of undeath, lichdom, and necromancy. Orcus commands legions of undead and seeks to extinguish all life, replacing it with his deathless servants.

## Domain in the Abyss

Thanatos, the layer of the undead — a frozen, lightless realm teeming with the risen dead.

## Relationships

Rivals Demogorgon for the title Prince of Demons. His domain overlaps with Vecna's necromantic arts, creating tension between their followers.
