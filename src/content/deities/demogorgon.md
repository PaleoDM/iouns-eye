## Description

Prince of Demons, embodiment of violence and rage. Demogorgon is considered the most powerful demon prince, though his two heads are said to constantly war with each other.

## Domain in the Abyss

The Gaping Maw, a layer of savage jungles and crushing seas.

## Relationships

Claims the title Prince of Demons, contested by Orcus and Graz'zt. His internal conflict between his two heads mirrors the chaos of the Abyss itself.
