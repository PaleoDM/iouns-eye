## Description

God of greed, hoards, and treasure. The Hoarder is the primary god of kobolds and a vassal of Tiamat, the Scaled Tyrant.

## Worship & Followers

Kobolds universally revere the Hoarder. His shrines are hidden in tunnels and warrens, surrounded by carefully counted treasures.

## Relationships

Serves Tiamat as a vassal. Bitter rival of Glittergold, the gnomish deity.
