## Description

A cursed lance recovered from Odaire, the Domain of Dread ruled by Maligno. The Curtain's Call allows its wielder to swap bodies with a target, but each use carries a small chance the switch becomes permanent.

## History

Freddie identified the weapon during the party's rest in the Shadowfell mists after escaping Odaire. A subsequent Legend Lore spell revealed the full extent of the curse. The lance's body-swapping power mirrors Maligno's own nature as a carrionette who seized control of living bodies.
