## Description

A talisman attuned to the Pallbearer's Domain of Dread, Potter's Field. When burned, the calling card summons the ghost ship Dancing Dirge, which can ferry travelers through the Shadowfell mists to the Pallbearer's domain.

## History

Claimed after the party's victory over Maligno in Odaire, the calling card served as the key to reaching Potter's Field. The party burned it at the docks to summon Captain Alistair and the Dancing Dirge, then covered themselves in leviathan blood to mask their living auras from the Pallbearer's detection.
