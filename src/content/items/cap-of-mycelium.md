## Description

A living fungal cap gifted to Thorn by the Great Self after the party restored the fragmented fungal colony in the Sparkworks tunnels. The cap grants a new proficiency and allows the wearer to speak with Eukaryotes.

## History

Thorn was elected by the party to serve as ambassador to the Great Self after they re-established the old truce: the colony would maintain the lights, alarms, and bridges of the Sparkworks District in exchange for protection from Underdark threats. The Cap of the Mycelium serves as the physical bond of that agreement.
