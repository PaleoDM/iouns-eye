## Description

A communication device used by the party for coordination. The sending crystal allows long-distance magical communication between its holders.

## History

Acquired during the party's operations in Rifthaven. The crystal was also connected to the Earthen Loam, as Boreal wore a pin matching the crystal and warned Thorn to destroy it if he valued his privacy.
