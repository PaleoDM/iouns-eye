## Description

Tyrell's pact weapon, which evolved into a Rapier of Warning after his eldritch patron expanded their pact. The rapier grants its wielder the benefits of the Warning enchantment, preventing surprise and granting advantage on initiative.

## History

The weapon was upgraded during the party's downtime after the liberation of Sparkworks, as Ty deepened his connection to his warlock patron.
