## Description

A locket given to Tyrell by Lulu, the celestial hollyphant who became his warlock patron after he banished the aberrant nautilus entity from his pact. The locket represents the bond between Ty and his celestial guide.

## History

After Ty made the decisive choice to embrace Lulu's guidance over the mustachioed nautilus aberration, Lulu became his primary patron, offering celestial powers and wisdom. Lulu accompanied the party through the Roc Run, Boughshadow, and into the Shadowfell, proving a steadfast companion.
