## Description

The third and final relic of Kareth Varn, recovered from the Breached Hollow in the Red Vale where Starspawn had infested the ruins.

## History

Recovered from the Breached Hollow (Session 8). Its return, alongside Verity's Edge and the Arbiter's Shroud, allowed Kareth Varn's oath-bound spirit to finally be laid to rest.
