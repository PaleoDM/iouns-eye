## Description

Kareth Varn's legendary spear, one of three relics scattered across the Red Vale battlefields after Zariel's forces impaled him.

## History

Recovered from the Braided Spear battle site (Session 8) where a long-dead oath-bound lieutenant guarded it. Returned to Kareth's resting place along with the Arbiter's Shroud and Dawncatcher, finally allowing the paladin's spirit to find peace.
