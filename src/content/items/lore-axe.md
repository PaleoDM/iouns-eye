## Description

A gift from Rosie, the guardian spirit of the Tree of Ambition, the Lore-Axe allows Thorn to speak for the trees. It was bestowed after Thorn protected the ancient crystal tree from Silverweave Ventures' extraction apparatus.

## History

When Rosie asked Thorn what he truly wanted, Thorn insisted that the seed does not have to want to be a tree. But Rosie pushed back: she was a spirit of the Tree of Ambition, and he must have some ambition. "Five is too few," Thorn decided. "Seven is better." With that declaration, Rosie gifted him the Lore-Axe.
